8.1. Election of a Vice-President of the European Parliament (to replace Ryszard Czarnecki)

The President announced that he had received nominations for Indrek Tarand and Zdzisław Krasnodębski (2018/2578(RSO))

(The list of Members voting is annexed to these Minutes (minutes of 1.3.2018 annex 1).)

The President proclaimed Zdzisław Krasnodębski a Vice-President of Parliament and congratulated him on his election.

In accordance with Rule 20(1), he took the place of outgoing Vice-President Ryszard Czarnecki in the order of precedence.
