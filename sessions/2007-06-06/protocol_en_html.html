3.  Statement by the President

The President informed the House that ETA had decided to end the ceasefire announced on 22 March 2006. On behalf of Parliament, he condemned the use of violence and called on all Member States to support Spain in its fight against terrorism.


4.  Approval of Minutes of previous sitting

The Minutes of the previous sitting were approved.
