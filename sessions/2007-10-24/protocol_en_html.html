The sitting opened at 9.00.

IN THE CHAIR: Gérard ONESTA
Vice-President

The following spoke: Martin Schulz , on behalf of the PSE Group , who called on the President of Parliament to condemn officially the anti-Semitic insults levelled at the Hungarian Prime Minister, Ferenc Gyurcsány , by participants in a demonstration in Budapest (the President replied that he would forward the request to the President of Parliament).

The following spoke: Graham Watson , on behalf of the ALDE Group , who thanked the President for organising the presentation of the Lux Prize at Parliament.


6.  Statement by the President

The President made a brief statement to mark the 62nd anniversary of the United Nations. He drew attention to the official launch at Parliament on the same day of the annual report on EU-UN cooperation on crisis management, development and promotion of democracy and fundamental freedoms.


7.  Welcome

On behalf of Parliament, the President welcomed members of a delegation from Mauritania - led by His Excellency Dr Abderrahmane Ould Tolba - who had taken their seats in the distinguished visitors' gallery.
