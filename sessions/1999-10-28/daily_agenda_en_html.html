. Draft amendments and proposed modifications concerning Section III (Commission) of the 2000 draft general budget.
. Draft amendments to Sections I, II, IV, V and VI of the 2000 draft general budget (European Parliament, the Council, the Court of Justice, the Court of Auditors, the Economic and Social Committee and the Committee of the Regions).
Under the Treaties, draft amendments (covering non-compulsory expenditure) require for adoption the votes of a majority of Parliament's component Members. Proposed modifications (covering compulsory expenditure) require only a majority of the votes cast.
