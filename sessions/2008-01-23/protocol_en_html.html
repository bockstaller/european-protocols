IN THE CHAIR: Rodi KRATSA-TSAGAROPOULOU
Vice-President

1.  Resumption of session

The sitting opened at 12.05.

The President announced that work could not begin until 12.30 because the Commission had been delayed.

(The sitting was suspended at 12.05 and resumed at 12.30.)


2.  Welcome

On behalf of Parliament, the President welcomed ACP members of the Bureau of the ACP-EU Joint Parliamentary Assembly and their delegations, who had taken their seats in the distinguished visitors' gallery.


3.  Approval of Minutes of previous sitting

The Minutes of the previous sitting were approved.


4.  Action taken on Parliament's positions and resolutions

The Commission communication on the action taken on the positions and resolutions adopted by Parliament at the October I and II 2007 part-sessions had been distributed.


5.  Texts of agreements forwarded by the Council

The Council had forwarded certified true copies of the following:

- agreement in the form of exchange of letters on the provisional application of the Fisheries Partnership Agreement between the European Community and the Republic of Mozambique;

- Fisheries Partnership Agreement between the European Community and the Republic of Mozambique.


6.  Proposals on energy and climate change (debate)

Commission statement: Proposals on energy and climate change

José Manuel Barroso (President of the Commission) made the statement.

The following spoke: Marianne Thyssen , on behalf of the PPE-DE Group, Hannes Swoboda , on behalf of the PSE Group, Graham Watson , on behalf of the ALDE Group, Liam Aylward , on behalf of the UEN Group, Rebecca Harms , on behalf of the Verts/ALE Group, Roberto Musacchio , on behalf of the GUE/NGL Group, Johannes Blokland , on behalf of the IND/DEM Group, Irena Belohorská , Non-attached Member, Karl-Heinz Florenz , Guido Sacconi , Lena Ek , Alessandro Foglietta , Claude Turmes , Esko Seppänen , Graham Booth , Jana Bobošíková , Gunnar Hökmark , Reino Paasilinna , Chris Davies , Guntars Krasts , Andreas Mölzer , Giles Chichester , Britta Thomsen , Vittorio Prodi , Bogdan Pęk , Péter Olajos , Riitta Myller , Francesco Musotto , Atanas Paparizov and Karin Scheele .

The following spoke under the 'catch-the-eye' procedure: Françoise Grossetête , Dorette Corbey , Avril Doyle , Inés Ayala Sender and Eva Lichtenberger .

The following spoke: José Manuel Barroso .

The debate closed.


7.  Dates for next sittings

The next sittings would be held on 30.01.2008 and 31.01.2008.


8.  Adjournment of the session

The session of the European Parliament was adjourned.

The sitting closed at 14.05.

Harald Rømer

Hans-Gert Pöttering

Secretary-General

President
