4. Agenda

The President pointed out that the Council and the Commission had made it known that they would not be attending the debate entitled 'Council and Commission statements on the state of play of the negotiations of the European Council concerning the economic crisis' (item 122 on the agenda), which had been added to the agenda during the previous day's discussions on the order of business (minutes of 24.10.2011, item 11). She therefore proposed changing the title of the debate to read: 'Statement by the President of Parliament on the state of play of the negotiations of the European Council concerning the economic crisis'. It would be followed by statements from the political groups.

The President established that there were no objections to the proposal.
