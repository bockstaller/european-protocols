6. Announcement by the President

The President announced that, under Rule 176(1), he had decided to censure Ioannis Lagos for having disrupted the sitting of 29 January 2020 by using abusive language and brandishing and then tearing up a piece of paper symbolising a national flag.

Mr Lagos had been stripped of his entitlement to the subsistence allowance for seven days and his participation in the activities of the European Parliament and its bodies had been suspended for four consecutive days, starting on 11 February 2020.

Mr Lagos had been notified of the decision on that day and was entitled to lodge an internal appeal against it with the Bureau in accordance with the Rules of Procedure.

16. Composition of Parliament

In accordance with the European Council's decision of 28 June 2018 and following the United Kingdom's withdrawal from the European Union on 31 January 2020, the competent authorities of the Netherlands had informed the European Parliament that Bart Groothuis, Dorein Rookmaker and Marcel De Graaff had been elected to Parliament with effect from 1 February 2020.

Under Rule 3(2), until such time as their credentials had been verified or a ruling had been given on any dispute, and provided that they had previously made a written declaration stating that they did not hold any office incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament, these Members would take their seats in Parliament and on its bodies and would enjoy all the rights attaching thereto.
