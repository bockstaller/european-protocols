6. Statement by the President

The President made a statement to mark the sixth European Day of the Victims of Terrorism. He paid tribute to the memory of the victims of terrorism in Europe, making particular mention of the victims of the terrorist attacks in Madrid on 11 March 2004 and in London on 7 July 2005. He denounced terrorist acts, describing them as an attack on democracy.


7. Calendar of part-sessions

The President announced that the Conference of Presidents had decided, in accordance with Rule 134(3) of the Rules of Procedure, to convene an extraordinary plenary sitting in Brussels on Wednesday 7 April 2010 from 14.30 to 16.30 on the European Council Report and Commission Statement on the results of the European Council Summit taking place on 25 and 26 March 2010.
