IN THE CHAIR: Josep BORRELL FONTELLES
President

1.  Resumption of session

The sitting opened at 17.00.


2.  Approval of Minutes of previous sitting

Antonio Tajani had informed the Presidency that he had been present at the sitting of 30.11.2005, but that his name was not on the attendance register.

Jean-Louis Bourlanges , Fausto Correia , Jan Mulder , Christopher Beazley and Marian Harkin had informed the Presidency that they had been present at the sitting of 01.12.2005 but that their names were not on the attendance register.

°
° ° °

The Minutes of the previous sitting were approved.


3.  Statement by the President

On the occasion of the anniversary of the adoption of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights, the President made a statement in which he condemned the use of the death penalty in certain countries, emphasising that it was contrary to the values of the European Union.


4.  Membership of Parliament

The Polish authorities had given notice of the appointment of Hanna Foltyn-Kubicka to replace Anna Elzbieta Fotyga as Member of Parliament with effect from 07.12.2005.

Josep Borrell Fontelles (President) drew attention to the provisions of Rule 3(5).


5.  Membership of committees

At the request of the ALDE Group, Parliament ratified the following appointment:

LIBE Committee: Giovanni Procacci .
