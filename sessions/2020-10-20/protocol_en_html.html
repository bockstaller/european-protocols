6. Amendment of the agenda

Following consultations with the political groups, the President proposed that the results of the third voting session should be announced that evening at 23.00 instead of the following day at 9.00.

In addition, the votes had been redistributed over different voting sessions based on the number of amendments and the number of requests for split and separate votes.

Information concerning the redistribution of votes over the different voting sessions was available on Parliament's website under the heading 'Priority information'.

Parliament agreed to this proposal. The agenda was duly altered.
