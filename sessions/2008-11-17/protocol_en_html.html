IN THE CHAIR: Hans-Gert PÖTTERING
President

1.  Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.00.


2.  Welcome

The President, on behalf of Parliament, welcomed participants of the EuroMedScola programme, who had taken their places in the distinguished visitors' gallery.


3.  Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.


4.  Composition of Parliament

The Italian authorities had given notice of the election of Romano Maria La Russa to a post incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament pursuant to Article 7(3) of the Act concerning the election of the members of the European Parliament by direct universal suffrage, and of the appointment of Antonio Mussa as a Member of the European Parliament in place of Romano Maria La Russa .

Parliament took note with effect from 04.11.2008.

The Maltese authorities had given notice of the appointment of Glenn Bedingfield as a Member of the European Parliament in place of Joseph Muscat, with effect from 29.10.2008.

The Slovene authorities had given notice of the appointment of Aurelio Juri as a Member of the European Parliament in place of Borut Pahor, with effect from 07.11.2008.

The President congratulated Borut Pahor on his election as Prime Minister of the Republic of Slovenia.

Under Rule 3(2), until such time as their credentials had been verified or a ruling had been given on any dispute, and provided that they had previously made written declarations stating that they did not hold any office incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament, Glenn Bedingfield, Aurelio Juri and Antonio Mussa would take their seats in Parliament and on its bodies and would enjoy all the rights attaching thereto.


5.  Verification of credentials

At the proposal of the JURI Committee, Parliament validated the mandate of Trevor Colman , with effect from 01.10.2008, and Jean-Paul Denanot and Michel Teychenné , with effect from 06.10.2008.


6.  Composition of committees and delegations

The President had received the following requests for appointments from the PPE-DE and PSE Groups:

Temporary Committee on Climate Change: Ivo Belet to replace Marianne Thyssen

Delegation for relations with the countries of south-east Europe: Marian-Jean Marinescu

Delegation to the EU-Kazakhstan, EU-Kyrgyzstan and EU-Uzbekistan Parliamentary Cooperation Committees and Delegation for relations with Tadjikistan, Turkmenistan and Mongolia: Jean-Paul Denanot

Delegation for relations with Mercosur: Michel Teychenné

The appointments would be deemed ratified if there were no objections before the approval of these minutes.

9.  Request for urgent procedure

Council request for the application of urgent procedure (Rule 134) for:

- Proposal for a Council regulation amending Regulation (EC) No 332/2002 establishing a facility providing medium-term financial assistance for Member States' balances of payments [COM(2008)0717 - C6-0389/2008 - 2008/0208(CNS) ]

Reason for request:

Developments in the context of international finance gave rise to the proposal to raise the threshold set by Regulation (EC) No 332/2002 in order to increase ther European Union's capacity to respond to possible needs of Member States outside the euro area.

Parliament would take a decision on the request for urgent procedure at the beginning of the following day's sitting.
