BRUSSELS

MINUTES

WEDNESDAY 29 NOVEMBER 2017

IN THE CHAIR: Mairead McGUINNESS

1. Resumption of the session

The session suspended on Thursday 16 November 2017 was resumed.


2. Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 15.04.


3. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.


4. Welcome

On behalf of Parliament, the President welcomed a delegation from the Irish Council of Churches, who had taken their seats in the distinguished visitors' gallery.


5. Composition of political groups

Sorin Moisă was no longer a member of the S&D Group, having left on 22 November 2017, and had joined the PPE Group as of 29 November 2017.
