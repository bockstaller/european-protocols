8. Award of the Sakharov Prize (formal sitting)

From 12.05 to 12.40, Parliament held a formal sitting for the award of the Sakharov Prize to Raif Badawi, the jailed Saudi blogger, for his fight for freedom of thought in Saudi Arabia.

The President called on the Saudi authorities to pardon and immediately release Raif Badawi.

In Mr Badawi's absence, his wife, Ensaf Haidar, received the prize on his behalf.

At Ms Haidar's request, Parliament observed a minute's silence for the victims of the terrorist attacks of 13 November 2015 in Paris.
