IN THE CHAIR: David Maria SASSOLI
President

1. Resumption of the session

The session suspended on Friday 10 July 2020 was resumed.


2. Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 9.33.


3. Announcement by the President

The President announced that following the conclusions of the extraordinary European Council meeting of 17-21 July 2020, he had decided, in agreement with the Conference of Presidents and in accordance with Rule 154(4), to convene an extraordinary sitting, today from 9:30 to 17:30 in order for the Parliament to debate and voice its position on the recovery plan and the Multiannual Financial Framework for 2021-2027.


4. Order of business

The schedule for the debate, together with the order of voting, had been distributed by email.

No changes were proposed.

The order of business was thus established.


5. Approval of the minutes of the previous sittings

The minutes of the sittings of 8 July 2020, 9 July 2020 and 10 July 2020 were approved.


6. Composition of Parliament

The German authorities had given notice of the election of Manuela Ripa to replace Klaus Buchner as Member of Parliament with effect from 16 July 2020.

Following the judgment of the Court of Justice of the European Union of 19 December 2019, and in view of the decision of Spain's Junta Electoral Central of 3 January 2020 and the decision of Spain's Tribunal Supremo of 9 January 2020, Parliament noted the election as a Member of the European Parliament of Jordi Solé i Ferrando with effect from 3 January 2020 to replace Oriol Junqueras i Vies, whose seat had been declared vacant with effect from 3 January 2020.

Parliament took note of their election.

Under Rule 3(2), until such time as their credentials had been verified or a ruling had been given on any dispute, and provided that they had previously made a written declaration stating that they did not hold any office incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament, Manuela Ripa and Jordi Solé i Ferrando would take their seats in Parliament and on its bodies and would enjoy all the rights attaching thereto.


7. Composition of committees and delegations

The President had received from the Verts/ALE Group the following decisions changing the composition of the committees and delegations:

ITRE Committee: Manuela Ripa

SEDE Subcommittee: Manuela Ripa

17. Explanations of vote

Written explanations of vote:

Explanations of vote submitted in writing under Rule 194 appear on the Members' pages on Parliament's website.

Exceptionally, explanations of vote of a maximum of 400 words would be accepted.


18. Corrections to votes and voting intentions

Corrections to votes and voting intentions appear on Parliament's website under 'Results of roll-call votes' as an annex to the minutes of the sitting. They are for information only and have no effect on the result of the vote as announced in plenary.

The document will be regularly updated in the two weeks following the part-session concerned.

After those two weeks, the list of corrections to votes and voting intentions will be finalised so that it can be translated and published in the Official Journal.
