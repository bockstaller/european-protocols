1. Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 8.35.


2. Statement by the President

The President made a statement condemning the terrorist attack the previous evening in Tunis in which 14 members of the presidential guard had been killed. He expressed Parliament’s condolences to the families of the victims, referring too to the recent terrorist attacks in Beirut, Paris and Bamako.
