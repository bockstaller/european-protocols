10. Statement by the President

The President made a statement on the situation in southern Algeria. He roundly condemned the taking of hostages and the killing of a British citizen. He expressed his condolences to the victim's family and expressed solidarity with the families of the hostages before stating that he hoped that the conflict could be resolved peacefully.
