The sitting resumed at 12.06.


4. Statement by the President

The President made a statement strongly condemning the murder of Maltese journalist Daphne Caruana Galizia. He announced that the Bureau had decided that the press room in Strasbourg was to be named after Ms Caruana Galizia. He assured her family, who were present in the gallery, that Ms Caruana Galizia's courageous work would not be forgotten.

Parliament observed a minute's silence.
