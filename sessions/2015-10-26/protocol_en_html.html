IN THE CHAIR: Martin SCHULZ
President

1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.05.


2. Statement by the President

The President made a statement on the coach accident in Puisseguin (France) on Friday, 23 October 2015 in which 43 people lost their lives. He offered Parliament's condolences to the families of the victims.


3. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

Alberto Cirio had been present at the sitting of 14 October 2015, but his name did not appear on the attendance register.

Siôn Simon stated that he had been present at the sittings of 12/01/2015 and 25/02/2015 but that his name did not appear on the attendance register.


4. Announcement by the President

The President announced that he had decided to take disciplinary measures against Janusz Korwin-Mikke and Gianluca Buonanno for disturbing Parliament's work on 7 July 2015 and 8 September 2015, and on 6 and 7 October 2015. The measures would comprise, firstly, the loss of the subsistence allowance for 10 days, and secondly, suspension from all the activities of Parliament and its bodies, also for a period of 10 days, in accordance with Rule 166. The Members concerned had been informed of the decision.

The President stated that Janusz Korwin-Mikke and Gianluca Buonanno had presented an internal appeal under Rule 167. The Bureau would rule on the appeal in the course of the evening.
