4.  Disputes over the validity of the mandates of several Members

During its meeting of 21.04.2005 and under Rule 3(2), the JURI Committee considered a number of cases in which the validity of Members' mandates had been disputed, and which had been referred to Parliament pursuant to Article 12 of the Act concerning the election of Members of the European Parliament by direct universal suffrage.

Having considered the issues involved, the JURI Committee unanimously recommended that Parliament:

- should deem inadmissible the objection from Stanisław Kocot, a Polish citizen, concerning the election of Janusz Lewandowski ,

- should deem inadmissible the objection from Friedrich Wilhelm Merck, a German citizen, concerning the election of Rebecca Harms , Heide Rühle , Angelika Beer , Gisela Kallenbach , Hiltrud Breyer , Elisabeth Schroedter and Helga Trüpel ,

- should deem unfounded the objection from Hans Kronenberger, an Austrian citizen, concerning the election of Andreas Mölzer .
