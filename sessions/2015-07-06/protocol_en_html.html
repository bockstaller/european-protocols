IN THE CHAIR: Martin SCHULZ
President

1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.05.


2. Statement by the President (Srebrenica commemoration)

The President made a statement on the occasion of the commemoration of the 20th anniversary of the Srebrenica massacre.

Parliament observed a minute's silence.


3. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.

The following spoke: Ivan Jakovčić to request that a motion for a resolution on Srebrenica be put on the agenda for Thursday (the President asked him to make the request when the order of business was being established).

The following spoke: Rebecca Harms, Gabriele Zimmer and Gianni Pittella on the President's position with regard to the referendum of 5 July 2015 in Greece.


4. Composition of Parliament

Jörg Leichtfried had given notice in writing of his resignation as Member of Parliament, with effect from 24 June 2015.

In accordance with Rule 4(1) and (3), Parliament had noted that the seat was vacant from that date and had informed the national authority concerned.


5. Composition of committees and delegations

The President had received the following request for an appointment from the PPE Group:

ACP-EU Joint Parliamentary Assembly: Davor Ivo Stier

The appointment would be deemed ratified if no objections were raised before the approval of these Minutes.
