IN THE CHAIR: Martin SCHULZ
President

1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.00.


2. Tribute

On behalf of Parliament, the President paid tribute to the memory of Lothar Bisky, former Chair of the GUE/NGL Group, who had died on 13 August 2013.

Parliament observed a minute's silence.


3. Statements by the President

The President made a statement on the train crash near Santiago de Compostela n 24 July 2013 and the coach crash near Naples on 28 July 2013, both of which had claimed many victims, and sent Parliament's condolences to their families.

He then spoke on the various projects to be concluded prior to the next European elections, which were scheduled for May 2014, including banking union and the negotiations on the Multiannual Financial Framework.

Lastly, he bade welcome to Vice-President Alexander Alvaro, and wished Vice-President Georgios Papastamkos a speedy recovery.


4. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.


5. Composition of Parliament

Following the death of Lothar Bisky, Parliament, on the basis of Rule 4(1), established that his seat had become vacant with effect from 14 August 2013.

The relevant Romanian authorities had announced:

- the appointment of Ramona Nicole Mănescu to the post of Minister of Transport of Romania with effect from 26 August 2013;

- the election of Eduard-Raul Hellvig to replace Ramona Nicole Mănescu with effect from 4 September 2013;

- the election of Ovidiu Ioan Silaghi to replace Cristian Silviu Bușoi with effect from 4 September 2013.

Pursuant to Article 7(1) of the Act of 20 September 1976 concerning the election of the Members of the European Parliament by direct universal suffrage, and to Rule 4(1) and (4), Parliament established that the seat of Ramona Nicole Mănescu had been vacant with effect from 26 August 2013 and noted the election of Eduard-Raul Hellvig and Ovidiu Ioan Silaghi with effect from 4 September 2013.

The Spanish authorities had given notice of the election of Iñaki Irazabalbeitia Fernández to replace Ana Miranda as Member of Parliament with effect from 11 July 2013.

Pursuant to Article 7(1) of the Act of 20 September 1976 concerning the election of the Members of the European Parliament by direct universal suffrage, and to Rule 4(1) and (4), Parliament noted the above with effect from 11 July 2013.

Under Rule 3(2), until such time as their credentials had been verified or a ruling had been given on any dispute, and provided that they had previously made a written declaration stating that they did not hold any office incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament, Eduard-Raul Hellvig, Ovidiu Ioan Silaghi and Iñaki Irazabalbeitia Fernández would take their seats in Parliament and on its bodies and would enjoy all the rights attaching thereto.
