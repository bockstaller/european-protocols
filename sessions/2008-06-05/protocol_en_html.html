4.  Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The following spoke: Monica Frassoni , who again raised the subject of the activation by the Commission of the European warning system on radioactive risks, following an incident at the Krško nuclear power station ( minutes of 05.06.2008, item 1 ) ; she asked the Presidency-in-Office of the Council and the Commission to uphold agreed levels of transparency and to clarify the situation, Roberto Musacchio , who supported her remarks, and Günter Verheugen (Vice-President of the Commission) , who described the events and the procedure followed by the Commission, and Rebecca Harms.

5.  Request for the waiver of parliamentary immunity

The Italian authorities had forwarded a request that the parliamentary immunity of Antonio Di Pietro be waived in a case pending before the Rome Civil Court, concerning events that had occurred when he was a Member of the European Parliament.

Pursuant to Rule 6(2), the request had been referred to the committee responsible, the JURI Committee.
