7.  Membership of Parliament

The Italian authorities had notified Parliament that:

- under Article 7(3) of the Act concerning the election of representatives of the European Parliament by direct universal suffrage, the office held by Corrado Gabriele was incompatible with that of a Member of the European Parliament; as a result his mandate as a Member of the Euopean Parliament would end with effect from 19 June 2006;

- Vincenzo Aita had been designated to replace Corrado Gabriele , with effect from 19 June 2006.

Pursuant to Rule 4(1), Parliament took note of this information.

The President reminded the House of the provisions of Rule 3(5).


8.  Dates for next sittings

The next sittings would be held from 03.07.2006 to 06.07.2006.


9.  Adjournment of session

The session of the European Parliament was adjourned.

The sitting closed at 13.30.
