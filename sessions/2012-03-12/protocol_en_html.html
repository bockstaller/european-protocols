 ATTENDANCE REGISTER


IN THE CHAIR: Martin SCHULZ
President

1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.00.


2. Statements by the President

The President made a statement about the situation in Syria in which he condemned the repression by the Syrian authorities. He called on President Bashar al-Assad to allow humanitarian aid for the civilian population to enter Syria.

On the occasion of the Day on Remembrance of the Victims of Terrorism, he recalled the attack in Madrid on 11 March 2004, and expressed Parliament's solidarity with the families of victims of all terrorist attacks.

He then spoke of the Fukushima disaster that had occurred a year ago.

Parliament observed a minute's silence in commemoration of the victims.


3. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.
