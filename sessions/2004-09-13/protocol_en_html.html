IN THE CHAIR: Josep BORRELL FONTELLES
President

1.  Resumption of session

The sitting opened at 17.05.


2.  Announcement by the President

The President announced that he would ask Parliament to observe a minute's silence at noon the following day to pay tribute to the victims of the hostage-taking in Beslan and all victims of terrorism. He stated that he would also refer to terrorism in his address to the House at 9.00 the following morning, and that he had asked the heads of state and government of the Member States to encourage all schools to join the tribute. The President added that, on behalf of Parliament, he had sent his condolences to the families of the victims of each terrorist attack. He also appealed to the hostage-takers in Iraq holding two Italians working for an NGO and two French journalists, and referred to the accident in which Patriarch Petros VII had been killed.


3.  Approval of Minutes of previous sitting

The Minutes of the previous sitting were approved.
