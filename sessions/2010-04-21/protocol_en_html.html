2. Parliamentary immunity

On 16 July 2009, the President of the European Parliament had received a request for the waiver of the parliamentary immunity of Sławomir Witold Nitras, submitted by a Polish citizen through his lawyer.

Having been asked his opinion on the admissibility of this request, the Chair of the Committee on Legal Affairs had informed the President of Parliament by letter of 17 April 2010 that, with respect to the Parliament resolution of 24 April 2009 on parliamentary immunity in Poland, this request must be deemed inadmissible.
