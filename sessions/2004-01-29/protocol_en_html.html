1. Opening of sitting

The sitting opened at 09.00.


2. Vote on request for urgent procedure

Request for urgent procedure:

- Proposal for a Council decision on the conclusion of an agreement between the European Community and the Republic of Croatia in the form of an exchange of letters on the transitional points system for heavy goods vehicles in transit through Austria [COM(2003) 833 - C5-0033/2004 - 2003/0319(CNS)] - RETT Committee

- Proposal for a Council decision on the conclusion of an agreement between the European Community and the Republic of Slovenia in the form of an exchange of letters on the transitional points system for heavy goods vehicles in transit through Austria [COM(2003) 835 - C5-0034/2004 - 2003/0320(CNS)] - RETT Committee

- Proposal for a Council decision on the conclusion of an administrative arrangement between the European Community and the Swiss Confederation in the form of an exchange of letters on the transitional points system for heavy goods vehicles in transit through Austria [COM(2003) 836 - C5-0035/2004 - 2003/0322(CNS)] - RETT Committee

- Proposal for a Council decision on the conclusion of an agreement between the European Community and the Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia in the form of an exchange of letters on the transitional points system for heavy goods vehicles in transit through Austria [COM(2003) 837 - C5-0036/2004 - 2003/0323(CNS)] - RETT Committee

The request for urgent procedure was approved for the four proposals for decisions.

The deadline for tabling amendments for plenary would be 05.02.2004 at 12.00.
