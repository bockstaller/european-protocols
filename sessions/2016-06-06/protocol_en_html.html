IN THE CHAIR: Martin SCHULZ
President

1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.00.


2. Tribute

On behalf of Parliament, the President paid tribute to the memory of Gianluca Buonanno.

Parliament observed a minute's silence.


3. Approval of the minutes of the previous sittings

The minutes of the sittings of 25 May 2016 and 26 May 2016 were approved.


4. Composition of Parliament

The Lithuanian authorities had given notice of the election of Laima Liucija Andrikienė to replace Gabrielius Landsbergis as Member of Parliament with effect from 30 May 2016.

Under Rule 3(2), until such time as her credentials had been verified or a ruling had been given on any dispute, and provided that she had previously made a written declaration stating that she did not hold any office incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament, Laima Liucija Andrikienė would take her seat in Parliament and on its bodies and would enjoy all the rights attaching thereto.

°
° ° °

Following the death of Gianluca Buonanno, Parliament, acting under Rule 4(1), established a vacancy with effect from 6 June 2016 and notified the Member State concerned.
