WEDNESDAY 7 JULY 2010

IN THE CHAIR: Jerzy BUZEK
President

1. Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 9.05.


2. Statement by the President

The President stated that it was the fifth anniversary of the terrorist attacks in London on 7 July 2005, and reaffirmed Parliament's steadfast condemnation of all acts of terrorism.


3. Corrigendum to a text adopted (Rule 216)

The President announced that he had received a corrigendum to the resolution adopted by Parliament on 17 June 2010 on the floods in central European countries, in particular Poland, the Czech Republic, Slovakia and Hungary, and in France (P7.TA-PROV(2010)0241), to the effect that Romania should be added to the list of countries affected by those floods.

Pursuant to Rule 216(4) of the Rules of Procedure, this corrigendum would be deemed approved unless, not later than 48 hours after its announcement, a request was made by a political group or at least 40 Members that it be put to the vote.

The text of the corrigendum was available on the Séance en direct site.
