IN THE CHAIR: David Maria SASSOLI
President

1. Resumption of the session

The session adjourned on Thursday 8 July 2021 was resumed.


2. Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 17.04.


3. Approval of the minutes of the previous sittings

The minutes of the sittings of 5, 6, 7 and 8 July 2021 were approved.


4. Request for waiver of immunity

The Greek authorities had forwarded a request to waive the immunity of Emmanouil Fragkos in order for criminal proceedings to be brought against him.

Pursuant to Rule 9(1), the request had been referred to the committee responsible, the JURI Committee.


5. Composition of political groups

Lucia Vuolo was no longer sitting with the non-attached Members and had joined the PPE Group as of 10 September 2021.


6. Composition of committees and delegations

The President had received from the PPE, ID and ECR groups the following decisions changing the composition of the committees and delegations:

- AFET Committee: Andrzej Halicki was no longer a member

- INTA Committee: Maxette Pirbakas was no longer a member

- ECON Committee: Fulvio Martusciello was no longer a member

- EMPL Committee: Lukas Mandl was no longer a member

- ENVI Committee: Annalisa Tardino was no longer a member

- ITRE Committee: Massimiliano Salini was no longer a member

- Delegation for relations with Bosnia and Herzegovina, and Kosovo: Ladislav Ilčić

- Delegation for relations with Canada: Ladislav Ilčić

- Delegation for relations with India: Lars Patrick Berg to replace Cristian Terheş

- Delegation for relations with South Africa: Cristian Terheş to replace Ryszard Antoni Legutko

- Delegation for relations with the Pan-African Parliament: Luisa Regimenti

- Delegation to the ACP-EU Joint Parliamentary Assembly: Giuseppe Milazzo

The decisions took effect as of that day.


7. Council position at first reading

The President announced that, in accordance with Rule 63(1), the following position had been received from the Council, together with the reasons which had led to its adoption, and the Commission’s position on:

- Position of the Council at first reading with a view to the adoption of a regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council establishing the Instrument for Pre-Accession assistance (IPA III) - Adopted by the Council on 7 September 2021 (06604/1/2021 - C9-0352/2021 - 2018/0247(COD))
referred to committee responsible: AFET

The three-month period available to Parliament to adopt its position would therefore begin the following day (14 September 2021).


8. Negotiations ahead of Parliament’s first reading (Rule 71)

The President announced, in accordance with Rule 71, the decisions taken by the ECON and ITRE committees to enter into interinstitutional negotiations on the basis of the following reports:

- ECON Committee: report on the proposal for a regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council on a pilot regime for market infrastructures based on distributed ledger technology (COM(2020)0594 – C9-0305/2020 – 2020/0267(COD)) – Rapporteur: Johan Van Overtveldt (A9-0240/2021).

- ITRE Committee: report on the proposal for a regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council on European data governance (Data Governance Act) (COM(2020)0767 – C9-0377/2020 – 2020/0340(COD)) – Rapporteur: Angelika Niebler (A9-0248/2021).

- ITRE Committee: report on the proposal for a decision of the European Parliament and of the Council on the participation of the Union in the European Partnership on Metrology jointly undertaken by several Member States (COM(2021)0089 – C9-0083/2021 – 2021/0049(COD)) – Rapporteur: Maria da Graça Carvalho (A9-0242/2021).

Under Rule 71(2), if, before midnight on the following day, Tuesday 14 September 2021, a request in writing had been received from a number of Members or one or more political groups reaching at least the medium threshold, the decisions to enter into negotiations would be put to the vote.

Negotiations may start at any time after that deadline has expired if no request for a vote in Parliament on the decisions to enter into negotiations has been made.


9. Calendar of part-sessions

The President announced that at the meeting of the Conference of Presidents of 9 September 2021, he had decided, together with the political group chairs, that an extraordinary part-session would be held on 27 January 2022 to honour the memory of the victims of the Holocaust.

That part-session would be held each year to mark the International Day of Commemoration in memory of the victims of the Holocaust.

The provisional revised calendar for 2022 was available on Parliament's website.


10. Signing of acts adopted in accordance with the ordinary legislative procedure (Rule 79)

The President announced that since the adjournment of the session on Thursday 8 July 2021 he had, together with the President of the Council, signed the following acts adopted under the ordinary legislative procedure:

- Directive of the European Parliament and of the Council amending Directive (EU) 2017/2397 as regards the transitional measures for the recognition of third-country certificates (00055/2021/LEX - C9-0317/2021 - 2021/0039(COD));

- Regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council amending Regulation (EU) 2019/833 laying down conservation and enforcement measures applicable in the Regulatory Area of the Northwest Atlantic Fisheries Organisation (00036/2021/LEX - C9-0316/2021 - 2020/0095(COD));

- Regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council on cross-border payments in the Union (codification) (00034/2021/LEX - C9-0315/2021 - 2020/0145(COD));

- Regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council on a temporary derogation from certain provisions of Directive 2002/58/EC as regards the use of technologies by providers of number-independent interpersonal communications services for the processing of personal and other data for the purpose of combating online child sexual abuse (00038/2021/LEX - C9-0313/2021 - 2020/0259(COD));

- Regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council on the public sector loan facility under the Just Transition Mechanism (00033/2021/LEX - C9-0312/2021 - 2020/0100(COD)).

The President announced that on Wednesday 15 September 2021, together with the President of the Council, he would sign the following act adopted under the ordinary legislative procedure:

- Regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council establishing the Instrument for Pre-Accession assistance (IPA III) (00067/2021/LEX - C9-0355/2021 - 2018/0247(COD)).
