1.  Resumption of session

The sitting opened at 17.00.


2.  Tribute

On behalf of Parliament, the President paid tribute to the memory of Rolandas Pavilionis, who had died on 10.05.2006.

Parliament observed a minute's silence.


3.  Approval of Minutes of previous sitting

Jean-Louis Bourlanges and Michael Gahler had informed the Presidency that they had been present at the sitting of 27.04.2006 but that their names were not on the attendance register.
