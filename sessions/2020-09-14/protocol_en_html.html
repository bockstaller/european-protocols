IN THE CHAIR: David Maria SASSOLI
President

1. Resumption of the session

The session suspended on Thursday 23 July 2020 was resumed.


2. Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 17.04.


3. Statements by the President

The President made a statement alluding to the health situation in Europe in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic and explained his decision not to hold the September part-session in Strasbourg. He thanked the Chairs of the political groups for supporting that decision, as well as his decision to re-introduce part-sessions in Strasbourg as soon as the health situation and security conditions permitted. He also thanked the French authorities for understanding the spirit of these decisions and for their cooperation. Lastly, he paid tribute to the Mayor of Strasbourg and the people of Strasbourg.

He also mentioned the case of the Slovak citizen Jozef Chovanec, who had died after being detained at Charleroi airport in February 2018. He recalled that human rights and the rule of law were fundamental values of the EU. He expressed his solidarity with Mr Chovanec's family and stated that the case was under consideration in the LIBE Committee.

⁂

The following spoke: Manon Aubry, Terry Reintke, Anna Fotyga and Nicolaus Fest.

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.

6. Requests for waiver of immunity

The competent authorities in Belgium had sent a request for Lívia Járóka's immunity to be waived in order for her to be summoned to appear before the police court in Brussels.

The requests had been referred to the JURI Committee, the committee responsible, in accordance with Rule 9(1).
