IN THE CHAIR: Hans-Gert PÖTTERING
President

8.  Statement by the President

At the request of the Conference of Presidents and on a proposal from the Middle East Working Group, the President made a brief statement welcoming the global agreement to end the crisis in Lebanon that had been concluded in Doha with the mediation of the Arab League and Qatar.

On behalf of Parliament, he invited all the parties to implement this agreement and welcomed the opportunity to renew relations with a Lebanese Parliament aware of its democratic rights and obligations.
