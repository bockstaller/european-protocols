IN THE CHAIR: Antonio TAJANI
Vice-President

1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 14.00.


2. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.


3. Formation of a political group

The President announced that the President of Parliament had been notified of the formation of the following political group in accordance with Rule 32(2):

The Europe of Nations and Freedom (ENF) Group had been formed with effect from 15 June 2015 and comprised 36 Members.


4. Composition of Parliament

The Polish authorities had given notice of the election of Edward Czesak to replace Andrzej Duda, as Member of Parliament with effect from 11 June 2015.

Under Rule 3(2), until such time as his credentials had been verified or a ruling had been given on any dispute, and provided that he had previously made a written declaration stating that he did not hold any office incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament, Edward Czesak would take his seat in Parliament and on its bodies and would enjoy all the rights attaching thereto.

°
° ° °

Emmanouil Glezos had given notice in writing of his resignation as Member of Parliament, with effect from midnight on 8 July 2015.

In accordance with Rule 4(1) and (3), Parliament had noted that the seat was vacant from 9 July 2015 and had informed the national authority concerned.

°
° ° °

Marit Paulsen had given notice in writing of her resignation as Member of Parliament, with effect from 30 September 2015.

In accordance with Rule 4(1) and (3), Parliament had noted that the seat was vacant from that date and had informed the national authority concerned.


5. Verification of credentials

On a proposal from the JURI Committee, Parliament confirmed the validity of the mandate of Pirkko Ruohonen-Lerner with effect from 27 April 2015.


6. Composition of committees

At the request of the ECR Group, the President ratified the following appointment:

JURI Committee: Edward Czesak to replace Raffaele Fitto.


7. Proposal for a Union act

The President announced that he had declared admissible the proposal for a Union act tabled by Richard Sulík in accordance with Rule 46(2), on amending Directive 2006/112/EC on the common system of value added tax (B8-0210/2015).

In accordance with Rule 46(3) the proposal had been referred to the ECON Committee as the committee responsible.


8. Corrigendum (Rule 231)

The ENVI Committee had forwarded the following corrigendum to a text adopted by Parliament:

- Corrigendum P8_TA-PROV(2015)0100(COR01) to the position of the European Parliament adopted at second reading on 28 April 2015 with a view to the adoption of Directive (EU) 2015/... of the European Parliament and of the Council amending Directive 98/70/EC relating to the quality of petrol and diesel fuels and amending Directive 2009/28/EC on the promotion of the use of energy from renewable sources (P8_TA-PROV(2015)0100 - 10710/2/2014 – C8-0004/2015 – 2012/0288(COD))

Pursuant to Rule 231(4), this corrigendum would be deemed approved unless, within 24 hours, a request was made by a political group or at least 40 Members that it be put to the vote.

The corrigendum would be available on Europarl for the remainder of the current part-session.
