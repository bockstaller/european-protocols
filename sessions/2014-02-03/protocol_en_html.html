5. Corrigendum (Rule 216)

The ECON Committee had submitted the following corrigendum to a text adopted by Parliament:

Corrigendum P7_TA(2010)0336(COR02) to Directive 2010/78/EU of the European Parliament and of the Council of 24 November 2010 amending Directives 98/26/EC, 2002/87/EC, 2003/6/EC, 2003/41/EC, 2003/71/EC, 2004/39/EC, 2004/109/EC, 2005/60/EC, 2006/48/EC, 2006/49/EC, and 2009/65/EC in respect of the powers of the European Supervisory Authority (European Banking Authority), the European Supervisory Authority (European Insurance and Occupational Pensions Authority) and the European Supervisory Authority (European Securities and Markets Authority) (OJ L 331, 15.12.2010, p. 120)

(position of the European Parliament adopted at first reading on 22 September 2010 with a view to the adoption of the above directive P7_TA(2010)0336) (OJ C 50 E, 21.2.2010, p. 212) (COM(2009)0576 – C7-0251/2009 – 2009/0161(COD)).

Pursuant to Rule 216(4), this corrigendum would be deemed approved unless, within 24 hours, a request was made by a political group or at least 40 Members that it be put to the vote.

6. Delegated acts (Rule 87a)

Pursuant to Rule 87a(6), the Chair of the Conference of Committee Chairs had informed the President of Parliament that no objections had been raised in respect of:

- a recommendation for a decision by the TRAN Committee to raise no objections to the draft Commission regulation amending Commission Regulation (EU) No 1178/2011 laying down technical requirements and administrative procedures related to civil aviation aircrew (D029683/02 - 2014/2500(RPS)) (B7-0071/2014);

- a recommendation for a decision by the REGI Committee to raise no objections to the Commission delegated regulation of 7 January 2014 on the European code of conduct on partnership in the framework of the European Structural and Investment Funds (C(2013)09651 - 2014/2508(DEA)) (B7-0086/2014).

If no objections to these recommendations are raised by a political group or at least 40 Members within 24 hours, they shall be deemed to have been approved. If a political group or at least 40 Members raise objections to them, they shall be put to the vote.

These recommendations are available on the Séance en direct website.
