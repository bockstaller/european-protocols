IN THE CHAIR: Hans-Gert PÖTTERING
President

1.  Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.00.


2.  Statement by the President

The President made a statement in which he expressed his delight at the fact that Íngrid Betancourt and 14 other hostages held by the FARC had been liberated by the Colombian army on 2 July 2008. He had passed on Parliament's congratulations to the people, Government and army of Colombia, and called on all terrorist organisations, including the FARC, to lay down their weapons and to release all hostages still captive.

°
° ° °

The President welcomed Jean-Pierre Jouyet, the French Minister of State responsible for European affairs, and expressed his satisfaction that the President-in-Office of the Council was present at a Monday sitting for the first time.


3.  Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.


4.  Composition of Parliament

The Romanian authorities had given notice of the appointment of Flaviu Călin Rus to replace Sorin Frunzăverde as Member of Parliament with effect from 24.06.2008.

Under Rule 3(2), until such time as his credentials had been verified or a ruling had been given on any dispute, and provided that he had previously made a written declaration stating that he did not hold any office incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament, Flaviu Călin Rus would take his seat in Parliament and on its bodies and would enjoy all the rights attaching thereto.


5.  Composition of political groups

Maria Grazia Pagano had joined the PSE Group.


6.  Verification of credentials

On the proposal of its JURI Committee, Parliament decided to confirm the validity of the mandates of the following Members of the European Parliament: Fabio Ciani , Roberto Fiore , Catiuscia Marini and Iva Zanicchi , with effect from 16.05.2008, Catherine Boursier with effect from 18.05.2008, Sebastiano Sanzarello with effect from 22.05.2008, Konstantinos Droutsas with effect from 03.06.2008, and Rapisardo Antinucci and Domenico Antonio Basile with effect from 17.06.2008.


7.  Composition of committees and delegations

The President had received the following requests for appointments from the PSE and ALDE Groups:

ECON Committee: Rapisardo Antinucci

REGI Committee: Maria Grazia Pagano

PECH Committee: Maria Grazia Pagano

Delegation for relations with South Africa: Marielle De Sarnez was no longer a member.

The appointments would be deemed ratified if there were no objections before the approval of these Minutes.
