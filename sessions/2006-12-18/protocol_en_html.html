IN THE CHAIR: Josep BORRELL FONTELLES
President

1.  Resumption of session

The sitting opened at 11.05.


2.  Approval of Minutes of previous sitting

The Minutes of the previous sitting were approved.

The following spoke:

- Jean-Marie Cavada (Chairman of the LIBE Committee), who thanked all those involved on 14 December 2006 in the adoption of ten proposals for decisions on the financing of the Area of Freedom, Security and Justice ( Minutes of 14.12.2006, Item 6.11 , Minutes of 14.12.2006, Item 6.12 , Minutes of 14.12.2006, Item 6.13 , Minutes of 14.12.2006, Item 6.15 , Minutes of 14.12.2006, Item 6.16 , Minutes of 14.12.2006, Item 6.22 , Minutes of 14.12.2005, Item 6.23 , Minutes of 14.12.2006, Item 6.26 , Minutes of 14.12.2006, Item 6.27 and Minutes of 14.12.2006, Item 6.28 ) and added that the texts now reflected the wishes of the House;

- Richard Corbett (rapporteur on the amendment to Rule 81 of the Rules of Procedure, adopted on 14.12.2006 - Minutes of 14.12.2006, Item 6.20 ), who asked that the new provisions should be made available in January 2007 (the President said a new provisional version of the Rules of Procedure would be distributed for January 2007).


3.  Documents received

4.  Welcome

On behalf of Parliament, the President welcomed members of a delegation from the Pan-African Parliament - led by its President, Gertrude Mongella - who had taken their seats in the distinguished visitors' gallery.


5.  Signing of REACH and the seventh framework research programme

Josep Borrell Fontelles (President of the European Parliament) and Matti Vanhanen (President-in-Office of the Council) made short statements following the adoption of the REACH programme and the seventh framework research programme ( Minutes of 13.12.2006, Item 8.1 , Minutes of 13.12.2006, Item 8.2 , Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item 8.10 , Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item 8.11 , Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item 8.14 , Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item 8.15 , Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item 8.16 , Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item 8.17 , Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item 8.18 , Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item 8.19 , Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item 8.20 and Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item 8.21 ) .

They then signed the acts, with José Manuel Barroso (President of the Commission) , Karl-Heinz Florenz (Chairman of the ENVI Committee), Guido Sacconi (rapporteur on the REACH reports), Miloslav Ransdorf (Vice-Chairman of the ITRE Committee) and the three rapporteurs on the seventh framework research programme, Jerzy Buzek , Philippe Busquin and Anne Laperrouze .


6.  Meeting of the European Council of 14 and 15 December 2006 - Activities of the Finnish Presidency (debate)

European Council report and Commission statement: Meeting of the European Council of 14 and 15 December 2006

Statement by the Presidency-in-Office of the Council: Activities of the Finnish Presidency

Matti Vanhanen (President-in-Office of the Council) introduced the European Council's report and made a statement on the six months of the Finnish Presidency of the Council.

José Manuel Barroso (President of the Commission) also made a statement on the European Council meeting.

The following spoke: Hans-Gert Poettering , on behalf of the PPE-DE Group, Martin Schulz , on behalf of the PSE Group, Graham Watson , on behalf of the ALDE Group, Brian Crowley , on behalf of the UEN Group, Monica Frassoni , on behalf of the Verts/ALE Group, Sylvia-Yvonne Kaufmann , on behalf of the GUE/NGL Group, Nigel Farage , on behalf of the IND/DEM Group, and Koenraad Dillen , Non-attached Member .

The following spoke under the catch-the-eye procedure: Alexander Stubb , Edite Estrela , Gérard Onesta , Gunnar Hökmark , Ville Itälä , Lasse Lehtinen , Józef Pinior , Margrietus van den Berg , Françoise Grossetête , Jan Mulder , Ryszard Czarnecki , Esko Seppänen , Piia-Noora Kauppi , Jacek Saryusz-Wolski , Marianne Mikko , Hannu Takkula , Zbigniew Zaleski , Richard Corbett , Kyriacos Triantaphyllides , Malcolm Harbour , Zbigniew Krzysztof Kuźmiuk , Simon Busuttil , Alojz Peterle , Tunne Kelam , Avril Doyle , Charles Tannock , Jacek Protasiewicz , Zita Pleštinská and Jerzy Buzek .

Matti Vanhanen and José Manuel Barroso replied to points raised by Members.

The debate closed.


7.  Dates for next sittings

The next sittings would be held from 15.01.2007 to 18.01.2007.


8.  Adjournment of session

The session of the European Parliament was adjourned.

The sitting closed at 13.25.

Julian Priestley
