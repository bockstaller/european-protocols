IN THE CHAIR: Martin SCHULZ
President

1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 15.05.


2. Statement by the President

The President made a statement condemning the double bomb attack in Ankara on 10 October which had left over 100 people dead and more than 500 injured and expressed Parliament's condolences to the victims' families. He underlined his concerns at the development of Turkey's internal situation and stressed in particular that free media were a cornerstone of democracy, and he called on all those involved to embark on a process of reconciliation.

With the agreement of the political groups, the President proposed the following modifications:

- A debate on the Council and Commission declarations on the 'Consequences of the European Court of Justice ruling invalidating the US Safe Harbour decision' would be added at 19.30 as the first item on the agenda after the votes.
