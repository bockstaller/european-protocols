EUROPEAN PARLIAMENT

2012 - 2013 SESSION

Sittings of 4 to 7 February 2013

STRASBOURG

MINUTES

MONDAY 4 FEBRUARY 2013

IN THE CHAIR: Martin SCHULZ
President

1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.00.


2. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.


3. Composition of Parliament

The Belgian authorities had given notice of the election of Mark Demesmaeker to replace Frieda Brepoels as Member of Parliament with effect from 1 February 2013.

Under Rule 3(2), until such time as his credentials had been verified or a ruling had been given on any dispute, and provided that he had previously made a written declaration stating that he did not hold any office incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament, Mark Demesmaeker would take his seat in Parliament and on its bodies and would enjoy all the rights attaching thereto.


4. Request for the waiver of parliamentary immunity

The Polish authorities responsible had asked the President for waiver of the parliamentary immunity of Jacek Olgierd Kurski, in a case pending before the Warsaw Public Prosecutor.

Pursuant to Rule 6(2), the request had been referred to the committee responsible, the JURI Committee.


5. Verification of credentials

On the JURI Committee's proposal, Parliament decided to confirm the mandates as Members of the European Parliament of Francesca Barracciu, with effect from 17 December 2012, Preslav Borissov, with effect from 1 January 2013, and Dan Dumitru Zamfirescu, with effect from 9 January 2013.


6. Composition of committees and delegations

The President had received the following requests for nominations from the GUE/NGL Group and the Non-Attached Members' secretariat:

- Delegation to the Parliamentary Assembly of the Union for the Mediterranean: Georgios Toussas to replace João Ferreira

- INTA Committee: Dan Dumitru Zamfirescu

- Delegation to the EU-Armenia, EU-Azerbaijan and EU-Georgia Parliamentary Cooperation Committees: Dan Dumitru Zamfirescu.

The appointments would be deemed ratified if there were no objections before the approval of these minutes.
