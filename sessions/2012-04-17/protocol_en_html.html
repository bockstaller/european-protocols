1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.05.


2. Croatian observers in the European Parliament

Speaking on behalf of Parliament, the President welcomed the members of the Croatian Parliament appointed to the European Parliament as observers, who had taken their places in the Chamber.

Their term of office began with effect from 1 April 2012.

The list of the names of the observers from Croatia is annexed to these minutes (minutes of 17.4.2012, Annex 1).
