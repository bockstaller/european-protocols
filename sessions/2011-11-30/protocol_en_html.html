IN THE CHAIR: Jerzy BUZEK
President

1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 15.00.


2. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.


3. Welcome

On behalf of Parliament, the President welcomed a group of human rights activists from Yemen, headed by Amal Basha, Ezzadine El Asbahi and Tawako Karman, who had taken their seats in the official gallery.


4. Statements by the President

The President made statements on his visit to Turkey the previous week, and the elections that had just been held in Egypt and Morocco.


5. Verification of credentials

On a proposal from the JURI Committee, Parliament validated the mandate of Kent Johansson, with effect from 21 October 2011.


6. Composition of Parliament

Elie Hoarau had given notice in writing of his resignation as Member of Parliament, with effect from 4 January 2012.

Under Rule 4(1) and (3) Parliament established the seat was vacant and informed the competent national authorities.


7. Signature of acts adopted under the ordinary legislative procedure

The President announced that on Wednesday, together with the President of the Council, he would sign the following act adopted under the ordinary legislative procedure (Rule 74):

- Regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council establishing a Programme to support the further development of an Integrated Maritime Policy (00055/2011/LEX - C7-0450/2011 - 2010/0257(COD))
