1. Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 9.00.


2. Announcement by the President

The President announced the release the previous day by the Ethiopian authorities of Merera Gudina, held in detention since November 2016 after having attended a meeting in Brussels at the Parliament. The President welcomed this decision.
