10.1. Election of a Vice-President of the European Parliament (vote)

The President announced that he had received nominations for Fabio Massimo Castaldo and Anneli Jäätteenmäki.

The candidates had indicated that they agreed to their names going forward.

The President decided that the election would be by secret ballot with electronic voting.

The vote took place.

The President announced the result:

Members voting       692

Votes cast          586

Abstentions       106

Absolute majority       294

Votes were cast as follows:

Anneli Jäätteenmäki       301 votes

Fabio Massimo Castaldo    285 votes

The list of Members voting is annexed to these Minutes (minutes of 20.5.2015 annex 1).

The President declared Anneli Jäätteenmäki elected to the post of Vice-President of the European Parliament and congratulated her on her election.

In accordance with Rule 20(1), she would take the place of her predecessor, i.e. 10th place, in the order of precedence.
