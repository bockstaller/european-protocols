2.  Vote on requests for urgent procedure

Request for urgent procedure:

- on the proposal for a Council regulation imposing certain restrictive measures in support of effective implementation of the mandate of the International Criminal Tribunal for the former Yugoslavia (ICTY) (COM(2004)0348 – SN 2057/2004 – C6-0041/2004 – 2004/0114(CNS) ) – Bourlanges report (A6-0006/2004 )

Jean-Louis Bourlanges , rapporteur , spoke.

The request was approved.

The deadline for tabling amendments would be Wednesday, 14 September 2004 at 12.00.

Vote: Minutes of 16.09.2004, Item 6.1

Request for urgent procedure:

- on the proposal for a Council regulation establishing an instrument of financial support for encouraging the economic development of the Turkish Cypriot community (COM(2004)0465 – C6-0098/2004 – 2004/0145(CNS) ) – Committee on Foreign Affairs

The following spoke: Elmar Brok , chairman of the AFET Committee, who suggested that the matter be dealt with via a report during the October II part-session, and Johannes (Hannes) Swoboda , on behalf of the PSE Group .

The request was rejected.

14.  Question Time (Commission)

Parliament considered a number of questions to the Commission (B6-0007/2004 ).

First part

Question 24 by Linda McAvan: Statistics on sudden unexplained death in young people  (H-0231/04 ) .

Joaquín Almunia (Member of the Commission) answered the question and a supplementary by Linda McAvan.

Question 25 by Athanasios Pafilis: Deep dismay over the use of drugs at the Olympic Games  (H-0249/04 ) .

Viviane Reding (Member of the Commission) answered the question and supplementaries by Athanasios Pafilis , Piia-Noora Kauppi and Dimitrios Papadimoulis .

Question 26 by Ryszard Czarnecki: Imports of genetically modified maize  (H-0253/04 ) .

Loyola de Palacio (Vice-President of the Commission) answered the question and supplementaries by Ryszard Czarnecki , John Purvis and Dimitrios Papadimoulis .

Second part

Question 27 by Saïd El Khadraoui: Liberalisation of port services  (H-0214/04 ) .

Question 28 by Bart Staes: Liberalisation of port services - current situation  (H-0218/04 ) .

Loyola de Palacio answered the questions and supplementaries by Saïd El Khadraoui , Bart Staes , Georgios Toussas .

Questions 29 and 30 would receive written answers.

The following spoke: Bernd Posselt and Rosa Miguélez Ramos , who were unhappy that their questions would not be answered at that point.

Question 31 by Georgios Papastamkos: Textiles and clothing after 2005  (H-0274/04 ) .

Pascal Lamy (Member of the Commission) answered the question and supplementaries by Georgios Papastamkos and Paul Rübig .

Question 32 by Brian Crowley: Placing the family at the heart of Europe's social agenda  (H-0221/04 ) .

Stavros Dimas (Member of the Commission) answered the question and a supplementary by Seán Ó Neachtain (deputising for the author) .

Question 33 by Dimitrios Papadimoulis: Problems concerning the transposition of Directive 1999/70/EC  (H-0229/04 ) .

Stavros Dimas answered the question and a supplementary by Dimitrios Papadimoulis .

Question 34 by Claude Moraes: Implementation of the transposition into national legislation of the Race Equality and Employment Anti-discrimination Directives  (H-0236/04 ) .

Stavros Dimas answered the question and a supplementary by Claude Moraes .

Questions which had not been answered for lack of time would receive written answers.

Commission Question Time closed.
