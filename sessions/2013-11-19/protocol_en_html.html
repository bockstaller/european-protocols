6. Statement by the President

The following spoke:

- Hannes Swoboda, to pay tribute to the former President of Cyprus, Glafcos Clerides, who had died on Friday;

- Giommaria Uggias, Francesca Barracciu, Claudio Morganti and Claude Turmes on the floods in Sardinia following cyclone Cleopatra;

- Jean-Marie Cavada to pay tribute to the two French journalists murdered in Mali, Ghislaine Dupont and Claude Verlon.

The President made a statement on the murder of the two French journalists in Mali. He paid tribute to journalists who are victims of war, persecution, oppression and terrorism throughout the world. He expressed Parliament's solidarity with the families of the two journalists.

The President concurred with the earlier speeches on the situation in Sardinia and expressed Parliament's solidarity with the families of the victims of the floods in Sardinia.

Parliament observed a minute's silence.
