IN THE CHAIR: Martin SCHULZ
President

1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.00.


2. Statements by the President

The President made a statement on the earthquake that had struck Nepal on 25 April 2015, killing 4 000 people and injuring over 6 500. He called on the international community to provide assistance to those affected and expressed Parliament's solidarity with the victims' families.

He also spoke with reference to the latest vessel carrying migrants to sink in the Mediterranean, on 21 April 2015, with the deaths of between 800 and 1 000 people.

Parliament observed a minute's silence in memory of the victims of the earthquake and of all those who had lost their lives in the Mediterranean.

The President ended with a statement on the nine people, including a French national, two Australians and an Indonesian, who were on death row in Indonesia. He called on the President of Indonesia to reinstate the moratorium on the death penalty and to abolish capital punishment in that country.
