IN THE CHAIR: Josep BORRELL FONTELLES
President

1.  Opening of sitting

The sitting opened at 9.00.


2.  Tribute

On behalf of Parliament, the President paid tribute to Renzo Imbeni, a former Vice-President of Parliament.

Parliament observed a minute's silence.


3.  Statement by the President

The President made a brief report to the House on the previous day's meeting between the EU Heads of State and Government, the Presidents of the Council, the Commission and the European Parliament, and George W. Bush, the President of the United States.


4.  Official welcome

On behalf of Parliament, the President welcomed Sam Rainsy, leader of one of the opposition parties in Cambodia, who had taken his seat in the distinguished visitors' gallery. The President also mentioned the human rights situation in Cambodia.
