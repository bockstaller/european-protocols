IN THE CHAIR: Hans-Gert PÖTTERING
 President

1.  Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.00.


2.  Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.


3.  Composition of Parliament

Graham Booth had given notice of his resignation as an MEP with effect from 01.10.2008.

In line with Rule 4(1), Parliament established the vacancy as of the above-mentioned date and informed the national authorities concerned.

The Italian authorities had given notice of the following:

- the end of the term of office at the European Parliament of Giuseppe Castiglione on account of incompatibilities within the meaning of Article 7(3) of the Act of 20 September 1976 concerning the election of the Members of the European Parliament by direct universal suffrage;

- the election to the European Parliament, replacing Giuseppe Castiglione , of Maddalena Calia, second on the relevant list of replacements, following the decision by the first person on the list, Michele Cimino, not to take up his mandate as an MEP.

Parliament took note, with effect from 12.09.2008.

Under Rule 3(2), until such time as her credentials had been verified or a ruling had been given on any dispute, and provided that she had previously made a written declaration stating that she did not hold any office incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament, Maddalena Calia would take her seat in Parliament and on its bodies and would enjoy all the rights attaching thereto.


4.  Verification of credentials

On a proposal from the JURI Committee, Parliament validated the mandates of the following Members: Paolo Bartolozzi and Erminio Enzo Boso , with effect from 23.06.2008, Elisabetta Gardini and Giovanni Robusti , with effect from 30.05.2008, and Maria Grazia Pagano , with effect from 17.06.2008.

Consequently, in accordance with Rule 116(4), it would be forwarded to its addressees and published, together with the names of the signatories, in the Texts Adopted of the sitting of 23.09.2008.
