IN THE CHAIR: Martin SCHULZ
President

1. Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 10.05.


2. Statement by the President

The President made an introductory statement, prior to the vote, in which he called on the other institutions to mobilise EU funds rapidly to aid the host countries to address the current migratory crisis.

The following spoke: Manfred Weber, on behalf of the PPE Group, Gianni Pittella, on behalf of the S&D Group, Guy Verhofstadt, on behalf of the ALDE Group, Philippe Lamberts, on behalf of the Verts/ALE Group, Ashley Fox, on behalf of the ECR Group, Neoklis Sylikiotis, on behalf of the GUE/NGL Group, Nigel Farage and David Borrelli, on behalf of the EFDD Group and Harald Vilimsky, on behalf of the ENF Group.

The following spoke: Frans Timmermans (First Vice-President of the Commission).


3. Voting time

Detailed voting results (amendments, separate and split votes, etc.) appear in the Results of Votes annex to the minutes.

The results of roll-call votes are available in electronic form only, as an annex to the minutes, and can be accessed via Europarl.


3.1. Council decision establishing provisional measures in the area of international protection for the benefit of Italy, Greece and Hungary* (vote)

(Simple majority)

COMMISSION PROPOSAL

Approved (P8_TA(2015)0324)

DRAFT LEGISLATIVE RESOLUTION

Adopted (P8_TA(2015)0324)


4. Explanations of vote

Written explanations of vote:

Explanations of vote submitted in writing under Rule 183 appear in the Verbatim Report of Proceedings for the sitting.


5. Corrections to votes and voting intentions

Corrections to votes and voting intentions appear on the Europarl website under 'Plenary/Votes/Results of Votes (Results of roll-call votes)'. They are published in hard copy in the Result of roll-call votes annex.

The electronic version on Europarl will be regularly updated for a maximum of two weeks after the day of the vote concerned.

After the two-week deadline has passed, the list of corrections to votes and voting intentions will be finalised so that it can be translated and published in the Official Journal.


6. Forwarding of texts adopted during the sitting

In accordance with Rule 192(2), the minutes of that day's sitting and of the previous day's sitting would be submitted to Parliament for its approval at the beginning of the next sitting.

With Parliament's agreement, the texts that had been adopted would be forwarded forthwith to the bodies named therein.


7. Dates of forthcoming sittings

The next sittings would be held from 5 October 2015 to 8 October 2015.


8. Adjournment of the session

The session of the European Parliament was adjourned.

The sitting closed at 10.30.

Klaus Welle

Martin Schulz

Secretary-General

President
