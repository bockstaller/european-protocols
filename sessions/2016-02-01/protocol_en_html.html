IN THE CHAIR: Martin SCHULZ
President

1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.05.


2. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.


3. Tribute

The President paid tribute to the memory of Miloslav Ransdorf, Member of the European Parliament.

Parliament observed a minute's silence.


4. Composition of Parliament

Following the death of Miloslav Ransdorf, Parliament, acting under Rule 4(1), established the vacancy of his seat with effect from 23 January 2016.

°
° ° °

The Portuguese authorities had given notice of the election of João Pimenta Lopes to replace Inês Cristina Zuber as Member of Parliament with effect from 31 January 2016.

Under Rule 3(2), until such time as his credentials had been verified or a ruling had been given on any dispute, and provided that he had previously made a written declaration stating that he did not hold any office incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament, João Pimenta Lopes would take his seat in Parliament and on its bodies and would enjoy all the rights attaching thereto.

°
° ° °

The following spoke: Birgit Sippel, to comment on what certain Members were purported to have said about migrants (the President said that responding to things said outside Parliament fell outside the scope of his competences).


5. Composition of committees

The President had received the following request for an appointment from the ECR Group:

Committee of Inquiry into Emission Measurements in the Automotive Sector: Julie Girling to replace Remo Sernagiotto

The appointment would be deemed ratified if no objections were raised before the approval of these Minutes.

8. Order of business

The final draft agenda for the February and I 2016 sittings (PE 576.422/PDOJ) had been distributed and the following changes had been proposed (Rule 152):

Monday

No changes.

Tuesday

Request from the ENF Group to include on the agenda, as the first item in the afternoon, a debate on 'The spread of the Zika virus'.

The following spoke: Joëlle Mélin, on behalf of the ENF Group, to move the request, and Enrique Guerrero Salom, to request that the matter first be referred to the competent committee.

Parliament approved the latter request.

The following spoke: Viviane Reding, to insist that report A8-0009/2016 on 'Negotiations for the Trade in Services Agreement (TiSA)', for which she was rapporteur, remain on the agenda for voting time on the Wednesday, Manfred Weber, to support what she had said, and Richard Corbett and Edouard Ferrand on the application of Rule 175.

Request from the GUE/NGL Group to insert, as a fourth item in the joint discussion on the Tuesday afternoon, statements by the Council and the Commission on 'Increased racist hatred and violence against refugees and migrants across Europe'.

The following spoke: Gabriele Zimmer, on behalf of the GUE/NGL Group, to move the request, Enrique Guerrero Salom, for the request, and Olaf Stuger, against the request.

Parliament voted by EV to approve the request (183 in favour, 142 against, 27 abstentions).

Pervenche Berès said that she would have voted in favour but had not had her voting card.

Wednesday, Thursday

No changes.
