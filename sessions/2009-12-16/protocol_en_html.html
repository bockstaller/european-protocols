12. Question Time (Council)

Parliament considered a number of questions to the Council (B7-0236/2009).

Question 1 (Bernd Posselt): Ethnic minorities in Serbia.

Cecilia Malmström (President-in-Office of the Council) answered the question and supplementaries by Bernd Posselt and Franz Obermayr.

Question 2 (Marian Harkin): Unemployment levels.

Cecilia Malmström answered the question and supplementaries by Marian Harkin, Andreas Mölzer and Paul Rübig.

Question 3 (Nikolaos Chountis): Negotiations for Turkey to take part in joint Frontex operations.

Cecilia Malmström answered the question and supplementaries by Nikolaos Chountis, Georgios Papanikolaou and Andreas Mölzer.

Question 4 (Seán Kelly): Climate negotiations in Copenhagen.

Cecilia Malmström answered the question and a supplementary by Seán Kelly.

Question 5 (Gay Mitchell): Climate change funding for the developing world.

Cecilia Malmström answered the questions and a supplementary by Gay Mitchell.

Question 6 (Ádám Kósa): Proposal for a Council directive on implementing the principle of equal treatment for persons irrespective of religion or belief, disability, age or sexual orientation.

Cecilia Malmström answered the questions and a supplementary by Ádám Kósa.

Question 7 (Vilija Blinkevičiūtė): Safeguarding the rights of disabled people.

Cecilia Malmström answered the question and supplementaries by Vilija Blinkevičiūtė, Christa Klaß and Mairead McGuinness.

Questions 8 and 9 were withdrawn.

Question 10 would not be taken as the subject to which it referred was already on the agenda of the part-session.

Question 11 lapsed as its author was absent.

Question 12 (Charalampos Aggourakis): Appalling murders of poor Peruvian farmers for profit.

Cecilia Malmström answered the question and a supplementary by Charalampos Aggourakis.

Question 13 (Ryszard Czarnecki): Discrimination against the Polish minority in Lithuania.

Cecilia Malmström answered the question and a supplementary by Ryszard Czarnecki.

Question 14 (Brian Crowley): Persecution of Buddhist monks and nuns in Vietnam.
Cecilia Malmström answered the question. Pat the Cope Gallagher (deputising for the author) spoke.

Question 15 (Pat the Cope Gallagher): Iceland's application to join the European Union.

Cecilia Malmström answered the question and a supplementary by Pat the Cope Gallagher.

Question 16 lapsed as its author was absent.

Question 17 (Mairead McGuinness): Biodegradable waste.

Cecilia Malmström answered the question and a supplementary by Mairead McGuinness.

Question 18 (Hans-Peter Martin): Successes by the Swedish Council Presidency in matters of transparency.

Cecilia Malmström answered the question and a supplementary by Hans-Peter Martin.

Question 19 was inadmissible.

Council Question Time closed.
