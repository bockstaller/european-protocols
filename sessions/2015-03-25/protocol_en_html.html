IN THE CHAIR: Martin SCHULZ
President

1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 15.05.


2. Statements by the President

The President made a statement on the air crash in the French Alps on 24 March 2015, in which 150 people had died, including a group of secondary school pupils from Haltern-am-See in Germany. On behalf of Parliament, the President sent condolences to the families of the victims of this tragic accident and to the families of the victims of the terrorist attack at the Bardo Museum in Tunis. On behalf of Parliament, the President condemned this act of violence.

Parliament observed a minute's silence.


3. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.

The following spoke: Bruno Gollnisch, who raised the question of his speech of Wednesday 11 March 2015 (minutes of 11.3.2015, item 9) (the President explained the current procedure).


4. Composition of Parliament

The Romanian authorities had given notice of the election of Mihai Turcanu to replace Eduard-Raul Hellvig as Member of Parliament with effect from 2 March 2015.

The Spanish authorities had given notice of the election of Estefanía Torres Martínez to replace Pablo Echenique as Member of Parliament with effect from 25 March 2015.

In accordance with Article 13 of the Act of 20 September 1976 concerning the election of the representatives of the European Parliament by direct universal suffrage and with Rule 4(1) of the Rules of Procedure, Parliament took note of the election of Mihai Turcanu and Estefanía Torres Martínez with effect from 2 March 2015 and 25 March 2015 respectively.

Under Rule 3(2), until such time as their credentials had been verified or a ruling had been given on any dispute, and provided that they had previously made a written declaration stating that they did not hold any office incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament, Mihai Turcanu and Estefanía Torres Martínez would take their seats in Parliament and on its bodies and would enjoy all the rights attaching thereto.


5. Verification of credentials

On a proposal from the JURI Committee, Parliament validated the mandate of Miguel Urbán Crespo.


6. Composition of committees and delegations

At the request of the GUE/NGL Group, Parliament ratified the following appointments:

ENVI Committee: Estefanía Torres Martínez to replace Pablo Echenique

Delegation for relations with the People's Republic of China: Estefanía Torres Martínez to replace Pablo Echenique.
