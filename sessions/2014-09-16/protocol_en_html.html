6. Voting time

Voting time was held with a live video link to the Verkhovna Rada (Ukrainian Parliament) in Kiev, which was required to vote at the same time as the European Parliament on the EU-Ukraine association agreement.

The President made a speech emphasising the historic importance of the agreement.

The President of Ukraine, Petro Poroshenko, made a speech, transmitted live on screen in the Chamber, in which he stressed Ukraine's vocation to be part of Europe.

Voting was opened simultaneously in Strasbourg and Kiev.

° ° °

Detailed voting results (amendments, separate and split votes, etc.) appear in the Results of Votes annex to the minutes.

The results of roll-call votes are available in electronic form only, as an annex to the minutes, and can be accessed via Europarl.
