IN THE CHAIR: Antonio TAJANI
President

1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 15.05.


2. Statement by the President

The President made a statement on International Girls in Information and Communications Technologies (ICT) Day, which was to be celebrated the next day. He emphasised that this initiative encouraged girls to follow studies and pursue careers in the technologies industry and promoted gender equality.


3. Welcome

On behalf of Parliament, the President welcomed a delegation of members of the parliaments of Myanmar and Sri Lanka, who were seated in the distinguished visitors' gallery.


4. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.
