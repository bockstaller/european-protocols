IN THE CHAIR: Martin SCHULZ
President

1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.00.


2. Statement by the President

The President said the situation in the Philippines following Typhoon Haiyan was still very serious. The European Union was committed to providing humanitarian aid to those affected and towards reconstruction in the region. He expressed Parliament's solidarity with the victims.

Parliament observed a minute's silence.


3. Welcome

The President welcomed 150 school children from 33 countries, who were taking part in the EuronestScola programme; they were seated in the public gallery.

°
° ° °

The following spoke: Davor Ivo Stier, recalling that it was the 22nd anniversary of the atrocities at Vukovar.


4. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.


5. Composition of Parliament

The competent German authorities had given notice of the election of Gabriele Stauner to replace Anja Weisgerber with effect from 30 October 2013, and of Hiltrud Breyer to replace Franziska Katharina Brantner with effect from 31 October 2013.

Under Article 7(1) of the Act of 20 September 1976 concerning the election of the Members of the European Parliament by direct universal suffrage, and in accordance with Rule 4(1) and (4) of the Rules of Procedure, Parliament took note of the election of Gabriele Stauner and Hiltrud Breyer with effect from 30 and 31 October 2013 respectively.

Under Rule 3(2), until such time as their credentials had been verified or a ruling had been given on any dispute, and provided that they had previously made a written declaration stating that they did not hold any office incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament, Gabriele Stauner and Hiltrud Breyer would take their seats in Parliament and on its bodies and would enjoy all the rights attaching thereto.


6. Request for the waiver of parliamentary immunity

The competent Polish authorities had sent the President a request for the waiver of the parliamentary immunity of Tadeusz Cymański.

Pursuant to Rule 6(2), the request had been referred to the committee responsible, the JURI Committee.
