IN THE CHAIR: Hans-Gert PÖTTERING
President

1.  Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.00.


2.  Statement by the President

The President made a statement on the death of Gilles Polin, a French EUFOR soldier, on the border between Chad and Sudan, and expressed Parliament's condolences to his family.


3.  Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.


4.  Request for the waiver of parliamentary immunity

The Polish authorities had forwarded a request to waive the parliamentary immunity of Witold Tomczak.

Pursuant to Rule 6(2), the request had been referred to the committee responsible, the JURI Committee.


5.  Composition of committees and delegations

The PSE Group had made it known that John Attard-Montalto was no longer a member of the Delegation for relations with Japan.
