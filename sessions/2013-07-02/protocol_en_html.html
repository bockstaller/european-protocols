(Secret ballot)

The President said that applications for the post of Ombudsman had been received from:

- Alex Brenninkmeijer

- Markus Jaeger

- Ria Oomen-Ruijten

- Dagmar Roth-Behrendt

- Francesco Enrico Speroni.

majority required: 318
