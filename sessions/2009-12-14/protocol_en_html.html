IN THE CHAIR: Jerzy BUZEK
President

1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.00.


2. Announcements by the President

The President condemned, on behalf of Parliament, the previous day's attack on Silvio Berlusconi, Prime Minister of Italy.

He referred to the twenty-eighth anniversary of the proclamation of martial law in Poland, and reminded the House that the award of the Sakharov Prize would take place on Wednesday 16 December.

° ° °

The following spoke:: Francesco Enrico Speroni, Gianni Pittella and Mario Mauro supporting, on behalf of their respective groups, the President's sentiments on the attack on Silvio Berlusconi.


3. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.


4. Request for the waiver of parliamentary immunity

The Public Prosecutor of the Republic of Poland had written to the President requesting the waiver of the immunity of Krzysztof Lisek in connection with a case pending before the Koszalin district prosecutor's office.

Pursuant to Rule 6(2), the request had been referred to the committee responsible, the JURI Committee.

6. Interpretation of the Rules of Procedure

The President informed Parliament, in accordance with Rule 211(3) of the Rules of Procedure, of the following interpretation of Rules 27 and 28 of the Rules of Procedure by the Committee on Constitutional Affairs, which had been asked to rule on the application of those provisions:

'In the absence of the Chair, the meeting of the Conference shall be chaired by the oldest Member, or, in the absence of the latter, by the oldest Member present.'

If this interpretation was not contested by a political group or by at least 40 Members (Rule 211(4)) before the minutes of the current sitting were approved it would be deemed to have been adopted. Otherwise, the matter would be put to the vote in Parliament.
