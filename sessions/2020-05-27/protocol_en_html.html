 ATTENDANCE REGISTER


IN THE CHAIR: David Maria SASSOLI
President

1. Resumption of the session

The session suspended on Friday 15 May 2020 was resumed.


2. Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 13.35.


3. Announcement by the President

The President recalled the preventive measures introduced at Parliament to enable social distancing during that part-session.


4. Approval of the minutes of the previous sittings

The minutes of the sittings of 13 May 2020, 14 May 2020 and 15 May 2020 were approved.


5. Composition of committees and delegations

The President had received from the ECR Group the following decision changing the composition of the committees and delegations:

Delegation for relations with India: Cristian Terheş to replace Raffaele Fitto

The decision took effect as of that day.


6. Signing of acts adopted in accordance with the ordinary legislative procedure (Rule 79)

The President announced that since the adjournment of the session on 15 May 2020 he had, together with the President of the Council, signed the following acts adopted under the ordinary legislative procedure:

- Regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council on the labelling of tyres with respect to fuel efficiency and other parameters, amending Regulation (EU) 2017/1369 and repealing Regulation (EC) No 1222/2009 (00003/2020/LEX - C9-0085/2020 - 2018/0148(COD))

- Decision of the European Parliament and of the Council on providing macro-financial assistance to enlargement and neighbourhood partners in the context of the COVID 19 pandemic (00011/2020/LEX - C9-0139/2020 - 2020/0065(COD))

- Regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council on minimum requirements for water reuse (00012/2020/LEX - C9-0130/2020 - 2018/0169(COD))

- Regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council amending Regulation (EC) No 1008/2008 on common rules for the operation of air services in the Community in view of the COVID 19 pandemic (00013/2020/LEX - C9-0144/2020 - 2020/0069(COD))

- Directive of the European Parliament and of the Council amending Directives (EU) 2016/797 and (EU) 2016/798, as regards the extension of their transposition periods (00014/2020/LEX - C9-0143/2020 - 2020/0071(COD))

- Regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council amending Regulation (EU) 2017/352, so as to allow the managing body of a port or the competent authority to provide flexibility in respect of the levying of port infrastructure charges in the context of the COVID-19 outbreak (00015/2020/LEX - C9-0140/2020 - 2020/0067(COD))

- Regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council laying down specific and temporary measures in view of the COVID-19 outbreak concerning the renewal or extension of certain certificates, licences and authorisations and the postponement of certain periodic checks and periodic training in certain areas of transport legislation (00016/2020/LEX - C9-0141/2020 - 2020/0068(COD)).


7. Order of business

The final draft agenda for that part-session (PE 653.005/PDOJ) had been distributed. No changes had been proposed.

The order of business was thus established.
