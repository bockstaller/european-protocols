4.  Verification of credentials

The President made the following announcement:

'On 15 November 2007, the judge hearing the application for interim measures at the Court of First Instance of the European Communities issued an order suspending the European Parliament decision of 24 May 2007 on the verification of the credentials of Mr Beniamino Donnici. It is, of course, my responsibility to apply this court ruling immediately.

This order rules that the legal situations of Mr Beniamino Donnici and Mr Achille Occhetto that existed before the adoption of the decision in question are re-established with effect from 15 November 2007.

Therefore, pursuant to this order and under Rule 3(2) of the Rules of Procedure, Mr Donnici takes his seat in Parliament and on its bodies and enjoys all the rights attaching thereto at least until the expiration of that order.

On a recommendation by the JURI Committee, I have asked the Legal Service to bring an appeal before the Court of Justice against the order of the judge hearing the application for interim measures at the Court of First Instance.'
