IN THE CHAIR: Hans-Gert PÖTTERING
President

1.  Opening of the session

Pursuant to Article 196, first paragraph, of the EC Treaty and Rule 127(2), the 2008-2009 session of the European Parliament opened.


2.  Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 9.00.


3.  Statement by the President

The President made a statement on the occasion of the European Day for the Victims of Terrorism, paying tribute to the victims of terrorism, the most recent being Isaías Carrasco, a former Socialist councillor in the Basque Country. The President again condemned acts of terrorism and expressed Parliament's solidarity with the families of the victims.

Parliament observed a minute's silence in memory of the victims of terrorism.


4.  Action taken on Parliament's resolutions

The Commission communication on the action it had taken in response to the resolutions adopted by Parliament during the November II 2007 part-session was available.
