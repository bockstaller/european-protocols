5. Statement by the President

The President made a statement on the attack on the American consulate in Benghazi, in which the American ambassador had been killed. He condemned this act of terrorism and expressed Parliament's solidarity with the US authorities.

IN THE CHAIR: Georgios PAPASTAMKOS
Vice-President

The following spoke: Ana Gomes, supporting what the President had said.
