IN THE CHAIR: David Maria SASSOLI
President

1. Resumption of the session

The session suspended Friday 17 April 2020 was resumed.


2. Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 15.03.


3. Statements by the President

The President made a statement to mark International Nurses Day and recalled that the World Health Organisation (WHO) had designated 2020 'Year of the Nurse and the Midwife'.

He paid tribute to health-care professionals for their commitment in the current situation and stressed the need to keep up efforts to ensure solidarity and find a coordinated European response to health crises.

The President recalled that the 70th anniversary of the Schuman Declaration had been celebrated on 9 May 2020 and stressed the importance of building true solidarity and undertaking a major process of reflection on Europe's future of Europe while providing immediate responses to current problems.


4. Announcement by the President

The President made a statement giving details of the preventive measures introduced at Parliament to enable social distancing during that part-session.
