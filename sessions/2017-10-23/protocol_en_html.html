IN THE CHAIR: Antonio TAJANI
President

1. Resumption of the session

The session suspended on Thursday 5 October 2017 was resumed.


2. Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 17.04.


3. Statement by the President

The President made a statement in the wake of the forest fires in Portugal and Spain. He announced European-level measures to prevent fires and support victims. He announced the decision of the Presidents of the Commission, the Council and Parliament to donate the amount received as part of the Princess of Asturias Award, awarded to the European Union in Oviedo, Spain, on Friday, 20 October 2017, to the victims.

Parliament observed a minute's silence in memory of the victims of the forest fires in Portugal and Spain and of Hurricane Ophelia in Ireland.


4. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.


5. Composition of Parliament

Ulrike Lunacek had given notice in writing of her resignation as Member of Parliament, with effect from 9 November 2017.

Pursuant to Rule 4(1), Parliament established the vacancy with effect from that date and informed the national authorities concerned.

°
° ° °

The United Kingdom authorities had given notice of the election of Rory Palmer to replace Glenis Willmott as Member of Parliament with effect from 3 October 2017.

Under Rule 3(2), until such time as his credentials had been verified or a ruling had been given on any dispute, and provided that he had previously made a written declaration stating that he did not hold any office incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament, Rory Palmer would take his seat in Parliament and on its bodies and would enjoy all the rights attaching thereto.


6. Election of a Vice-President of the European Parliament (submission of applications)

Following the resignation of Ulrike Lunacek from her post as Vice-President as of 24 October 2017 and at the proposal of the President, the deadline for the submission of applications for the election of a Vice-President had been set at 18:00 on Tuesday, 24 October 2017.

The election would take place at 12:00 on Thursday, 26 October 2017.
