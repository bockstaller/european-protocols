IN THE CHAIR: Josep BORRELL FONTELLES
President

1.  Resumption of session

The sitting opened at 15.00.


2.  Tribute

On behalf of Parliament, the President paid tribute to the memory of Antoni Gutiérrez Díaz – a Member of the European Parliament between 1987 and 1999 and Vice-President between 1992 and 1994 – who had died on 6 October 2006.

Parliament observed a minute's silence.


3.  Statement by the President

The President made a statement on the murder of the Russian journalist Anna Politkovskaya in Moscow on 7 October 2006. He pointed out that he had called on the Russian authorities to do everything within their power to find out who was responsible for the murder. He added that he had passed on Parliament's condolences to Mrs Politkovskaya's family and friends. Finally, the President pointed out that 63 journalists had been killed across the world since the beginning of the year and that 120 journalists were in prison.

Parliament observed a minute's silence.


4.  Approval of Minutes of previous sitting

The Minutes of the previous sitting were approved.

9.  Membership of Parliament

Cecilia Malmström had given notice of her appointment as Minister for European Affairs in the Swedish Government, with effect from 06.10.2006.

Since, pursuant to Article 7(1) of the Act concerning the election of representatives of the European Parliament by direct universal suffrage, this office was incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament, Parliament established under Rule 4(4) of its Rules of Procedure that the vacancy would take effect from 06.10.2006 and informed the Member State concerned thereof.

The Estonian authorities had given notice of the appointment of Katrin Saks to replace Toomas Hendrik Ilves as Member of Parliament with effect from 09.10.2006.

Parliament noted the appointment under Rule 4(1).

The President reminded the House of the provisions of Rule 3(5).

Observers

In accordance with the rules laid down by the Bureau, Parliament would inform the Romanian authorities thereof.
