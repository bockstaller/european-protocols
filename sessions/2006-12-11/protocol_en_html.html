IN THE CHAIR: Josep BORRELL FONTELLES
President

1.  Resumption of session

The sitting opened at 17.00.


2.  Approval of Minutes of previous sitting

The Minutes of the previous sitting were approved.

Margrietus van den Berg spoke on the Commission statement on AIDS ( Minutes of 30.11.2006, Item 4 ) .


3.  Membership of Parliament (Observers)

The Chairman of the Bulgarian National Assembly had given notice of the designation of Konstantin Dimitrov as an Observer at the European Parliament with effect from 01.12.2006, to replace Dimitar Abadjiev.


4.  Request for the defence of parliamentary immunity

Giuseppe Gargani had requested that his parliamentary immunity be upheld in the context of a case being heard before a court in Rome.

Under Rule 6(3), the request had been referred to the committee responsible, the JURI Committee.
