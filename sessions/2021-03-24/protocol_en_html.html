IN THE CHAIR: David Maria SASSOLI
President

1. Resumption of the session

The session suspended on Thursday 11 March 2021 was resumed.


2. Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 15.07.


3. Statements by the President

The President made a statement on the sanctions that China had imposed on five MEPs and on Parliament’s Subcommittee on Human Rights. He declared that the sanctions were an unacceptable response to Parliament’s legitimate concerns about human rights. He expressed his support for the five MEPs concerned and for the EU bodies that had been unjustly accused. He maintained once again that Parliament would continue to defend human rights and the rule of law.

The following spoke: Reinhard Bütikofer (Chair of the Delegation for relations with the People’s Republic of China) and Maria Arena (Chair of the Subcommittee on Human Rights).

⁂

The President made a statement to mark the 200th anniversary the following day, 25 March 2021, of the start of the pro-independence revolution that led to the foundation of a sovereign Greek state in 1830.

⁂

The President made a statement to mark the anniversary of the mass deportations of people from the Baltic countries to Siberia on 25 March 1949.

Parliament observed a minute’s silence.


4. Approval of the minutes of the previous sittings

The minutes of the sittings of 8, 9, 10 and 11 March 2021 were approved.
