MINUTES

THURSDAY 25 MARCH 2010

IN THE CHAIR: Jerzy BUZEK
President

1. Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 9.10.


2. Statements by the President

The President made a statement on the killing of the French policeman Jean-Serge Nérin by ETA on 16 March 2010. He conveyed his and Parliament's condolences to the victim's family.

The President made a statement on the arrest in Cuba of 30 participants in a demonstration by the 'Ladies in White' group. That group had been awarded the Sakharov Prize in 2005.
