1.  Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.05.


2.  Statement by the President

The President made a statement on the earthquake in Abruzzo and, on behalf of Parliament, expressed his condolences to the families of the victims.

Parliament observed a minute's silence in memory of the victims.


3.  Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.

4.  Verification of credentials

At the proposal of the JURI Committee, Parliament validated the mandate of Bernard Soulage , with effect from 1 February 2009, and Ioan Lucian Hămbăşan , with effect from 1 March 2009.


5.  The 'Say NO to Violence against Women' campaign (written declaration)

Written Declaration 0094/2008 by Eva-Britt Svensson on the 'Say NO to Violence against Women' campaign had been signed by the majority of Parliament's component Members. Consequently, in accordance with Rule 116(4), it would be forwarded to its addressees and published, together with the names of the signatories, in the Texts Adopted of the sitting of 22 April 2009.
