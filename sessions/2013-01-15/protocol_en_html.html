1. Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 9.05.


2. Agenda

The President proposed the following changes to the agenda under Rule 140(2):

- placing on the agenda for that afternoon, at 16.30, of a statement by Catherine Ashton (High Representative of the Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy and Vice-President of the Commission) on the situation in Mali, followed by a debate without the tabling of motions for resolution;

- placing on the agenda for Thursday, as the first item that day, of the debate on the oral question 'One carry-on bag' rule imposed by certain airlines (item 128 on the agenda);

The President said that no one had objected to those proposals.
