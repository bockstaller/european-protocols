IN THE CHAIR: Martin SCHULZ
President

1. Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 9.00.

The President reminded those present of the provisions of Rule 14(2).


2. Brief presentations by candidates for the post of President

The President noted that he had received applications from Eleonora Forenza, Jean Lambert, Gianni Pittella, Laurenţiu Rebega, Helga Stevens and Antonio Tajani (minutes of 16.1.2017, item 10), in accordance with Rule 15(1). He added that Guy Verhofstadt had withdrawn his application.

Laurenţiu Rebega, Jean Lambert, Eleonora Forenza, Helga Stevens, Gianni Pittella and Antonio Tajani presented their candidatures.


3. Election of the President of Parliament (First ballot)

The President noted that, pursuant to Rule 16(1), the President would be elected by a majority of the votes cast, and that invalid votes would not be counted.

Lots were then drawn to appoint eight tellers. The following Members were appointed: António Marinho e Pinto, Christian Ehler, Anneliese Dodds, Ricardo Serrão Santos, György Hölvényi, Lola Sánchez Caldentey, Gunnar Hökmark and Elisabeth Köstinger.

The President informed the Members of the voting procedure.

The vote was taken.

(The sitting was suspended at 10.20 for the votes to be counted, and resumed at 11.25).


4. Election of the President of Parliament (First ballot) (announcement of results)

The President announced the results of the vote:

- Members voting:       718

- Blank or void:       35

- Votes cast:         683

- Absolute majority:      342

(The list of Members voting is annexed to these Minutes.) (minutes of 17.1.2017 annex 1)

Votes received:

Eleonora Forenza:       50

Jean Lambert:       56

Gianni Pittella:      183

Laurenţiu Rebega:       43

Helga Stevens:       77

Antonio Tajani:      274

Since no candidate had obtained an absolute majority a second ballot was required.

The President announced that none of the candidates from the first round had withdrawn and that no additional applications had so far been received.

The deadline for submitting new applications was 12:30.

(The sitting was suspended at 11.30 and resumed at 13.00.)


5. Election of the President of Parliament (Second ballot)

The President announced that no new applications had been submitted and that the same candidates as in the first ballot were standing.

The list of tellers remained the same.

The vote was taken.

(The sitting was suspended at 13.30 for the votes to be counted, and resumed at 14.35).


6. Election of the President of Parliament (Second ballot) (announcement of results)

The President read out the results of the ballot:

- Members voting: 725

- Blank or void: 34

- Votes cast: 691

- Absolute majority: 346

(The list of Members voting is annexed to these Minutes.)(minutes of 17.1.2017 annex 2)

Votes received:

Eleonora Forenza: 42

Jean Lambert: 51

Gianni Pittella: 200

Laurenţiu Rebega: 45

Helga Stevens: 66

Antonio Tajani: 287

Since no candidate had obtained an absolute majority a third ballot was required.

The President stated that the list of candidates would be the same as for the second ballot and that no new applications to stand had so far been submitted.

The deadline for submitting or withdrawing applications was set at 17.00.

(The sitting was suspended at 14.40 and resumed at 17.30.)


7. Election of the President of Parliament (third ballot)

The President announced that no new applications had been submitted and that the same candidates as in the second ballot were standing.

The list of tellers remained the same.

The vote was taken.

The President called Marcus Pretzell to order for statements made off microphone.

(The sitting was suspended at 18.00 for the votes to be counted, and resumed at 19.00).


8. Election of the President of Parliament (third ballot) (announcement of results)

The President announced that, during the third ballot, a Member had refused to show his identity document and placed his ballot paper next to the ballot box and not in it. The Committee of Tellers had therefore unanimously decided to declare his vote invalid.

The President read out the results of the ballot:

- Members voting: 719

- Blank or void: 29

- Votes cast: 690

- Absolute majority: 346

(The list of Members voting is annexed to these Minutes.)(minutes of 17.1.2017 annex 3)

Votes received:

Eleonora Forenza: 45

Jean Lambert: 53

Gianni Pittella: 199

Laurenţiu Rebega: 44

Helga Stevens: 58

Antonio Tajani: 291

Since no candidate had obtained an absolute majority a fourth ballot was required.

The President pointed out that, in accordance with Article 16(1), the fourth ballot was confined to the two Members having obtained the highest number of votes in the third ballot. These two Members were Gianni Pittella and Antonio Tajani.

(The sitting was suspended at 19.10 and resumed at 20.00.)


9. Election of the President of Parliament (fourth ballot)

Lara Comi was appointed teller, replacing Anneliese Dodds.

The President reminded members of the provisions of Rule 16(1).

The vote was taken.

(The sitting was suspended at 20.25 for the votes to be counted, and resumed at 21.00).


10. Election of the President of Parliament (fourth ballot) (announcement of results)

The President read out the results of the ballot:

- Members voting: 713

- Blank or void: 80

- Votes cast: 633

Votes received:

Gianni Pittella: 282

Antonio Tajani: 351

(The list of Members voting is annexed to these Minutes.)(minutes of 17.1.2017 annex 4)

Since Antonio Tajani had obtained a majority, Martin Schulz declared him President of the European Parliament and congratulated him on his election.

IN THE CHAIR: Antonio TAJANI
President

The President made a statement on the occasion of his election.


11. Election of the Vice-Presidents of Parliament (deadline for submitting nominations)

On a proposal from the The President, the deadline for submitting nominations for the election of the Vice-Presidents would be 22:30. The election would be held at 9.00 on Wednesday, 18 January 2017.


12. Agenda of the next sitting

The agenda of the next day's sitting had been established ('Agenda' PE 596.857/PDOJ).


13. Closure of the sitting

The sitting closed at 21.10.

Klaus Welle

Martin Schulz

Secretary-General

President
