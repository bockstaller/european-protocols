IN THE CHAIR: Jerzy BUZEK
President

1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.05.


2. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.


3. Announcements by the President

The President welcomed the award of the 2010 Nobel Peace Prize to the Chinese dissident Liu Xiaobo. He called on the Chinese authorities to free Liu Xiaobo and other human rights activists, and spoke of Parliament's commitment to human rights.

The President added that the International Day for the Eradication of Poverty had been celebrated on the previous day. Today, it was the fourth European Anti-Trafficking Day. On Tuesday, Ban Ki-moon, Secretary-General of the United Nations, would be visiting Parliament to commemorate the Day for the Eradication of Poverty.

The President said that he would deliver his mid-term address on Wednesday 20 October 2010 (12.10-12.30).


4. Signature of acts adopted under the ordinary legislative procedure

The President announced that on Wednesday, together with the President of the Council, he would sign the following acts adopted under the ordinary legislative procedure (Rule 74):

- Directive of the European Parliament and of the Council on reporting formalities for ships arriving in and/or departing from ports of the Member States and repealing Directive 2002/6/EC (00034/2010/LEX - C7-0329/2010 - 2009/0005(COD))
