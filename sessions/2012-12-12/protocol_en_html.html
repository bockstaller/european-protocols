1. Opening of the sitting

The sitting opened at 9.05.


2. Nobel Peace Prize ceremony

On the occasion of the award of the 2012 Nobel Peace Prize to the European Union, the President and José Manuel Barroso (President of the Commission) made a short speech highlighting the symbolic importance of the event.

The President then symbolically handed the prize to 20 representatives of the citizens of the European Union in the presence of Andreas Mavroyiannis (President-in-Office of the Council) and José Manuel Barroso.


3. Composition of political groups

6. Award of the Sakharov Prize (formal sitting)

From 12.05 to 12.50 Parliament held a formal sitting for the award of the Sakharov Prize for Freedom of Thought to the Iranians Nasrin Sotoudeh, lawyer and human rights advocate, and Jafar Panahi, film director.

As they were unable to leave Iran, the two prize winners were represented by Karim Lahidji, Solmaz Panahi and Serge Toubiana, and by Shirin Ebadi and Costa Gravas, who addressed the House on behalf of Nasrin Sotoudeh and Jafar Panahi.

Detailed voting results (amendments, separate and split votes, etc.) appear in the Results of Votes annex to the minutes.

11. Annual report on human rights and democracy in the world 2011 and the European Union's policy on the matter - EU's human rights strategy (debate)
