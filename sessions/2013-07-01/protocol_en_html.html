IN THE CHAIR: Martin SCHULZ
President

1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.15.


2. Welcome

On behalf of Parliament, the President welcomed Josip Leko, Speaker of the Croatian Parliament, the Sabor, who had taken a seat in the distinguished visitors' gallery.


3. Formal sitting - Welcoming of the Croatian Members

From 17.20 to 17.25, a formal sitting of Parliament was held on the occasion of the Croatian Members taking up office.

The President made a statement welcoming the twelve new Members of the European Parliament appointed by Croatia.


4. Statements by the President

The President made a statement on the violent demonstrations in Cairo that had resulted in at least 16 deaths, and conveyed Parliament's condolences to the families of the victims.

°
° ° °

The President paid tribute to Emilio Colombo, a former European Parliament President, who had died on 24 June 2013.

°
° ° °

The President spoke of the accusations in the European press about the bugging by the US authorities of EU institutional buildings and said that, if the accusations proved true, he considered such practices to be utterly unacceptable.


5. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.

In accordance with Article 7(1) of the Act concerning the election of the Members of the European Parliament by direct universal suffrage of 20 September 1976 and with Rule 4(1) and (4), Parliament took note of their election with effect from 1 July 2013.

Under Rule 3(2), until such time as their credentials had been verified or a ruling had been given on any dispute, and provided that they had previously made a written declaration stating that they did not hold any office incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament, Marino Baldini, Biljana Borzan, Zdravka Bušić, Ivana Maletić, Sandra Petrović Jakovina, Tonino Picula, Andrej Plenković, Davor Stier, Dubravka Šuica, Ruža Tomašić, Oleg Valjalo and Nikola Vuljanić would take their seats in Parliament and on its bodies and would enjoy all the rights attaching thereto.

°
° ° °

Cristian Silviu Buşoi had given notice in writing of his resignation as Member of Parliament, with effect from 28 June 2013..

In accordance with Rule 4(3), Parliament had established the vacancy and informed the national authority concerned.


7. Verification of credentials

On a proposal from the JURI Committee, Parliament had decided to confirm the validity of the mandate of Marusya Lyubcheva, with effect from 7 June 2013.

9. Corrigendum (Rule 216)

The IMCO Committee had forwarded the following corrigendum to a text adopted by the European Parliament:

Corrigendum P6_TA(2009)0360(COR01) to Directive 2009/136/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of 25 November 2009 amending Directive 2002/22/EC on universal service and users' rights relating to electronic communications networks and services, Directive 2002/58/EC concerning the processing of personal data and the protection of privacy in the electronic communications sector and Regulation (EC) No 2006/2004 on cooperation between national authorities responsible for the enforcement of consumer protection laws (OJ L 337, 18.12.2009, p. 11) (position of the European Parliament adopted at second reading on 6 May 2009 with a view to the adoption of the above directive P6_TA(2009)0360 (OJ C 212 E, 5.8.2010, p. 260)) (16497/1/2008 – C6-0068/2009 – 2007/0248(COD)).

Pursuant to Rule 216(4), this corrigendum would be deemed approved unless, within 24 hours, a request was made by a political group or at least 40 Members that it be put to the vote.

The corrigendum was available on the Séance en direct site during the July part-session..
