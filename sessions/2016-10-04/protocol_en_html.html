2. Amendment of the agenda

The President proposed, after consultation with the political groups, that the following five motions for resolutions from the ENVI Committee be taken at Voting Time on Thursday 6 October 2016:

—   Objection pursuant to Rule 106: Placing on the market for cultivation of genetically modified maize Bt11 (2016/2919(RSP)) (B8-1083/2016);

—   Objection pursuant to Rule 106: Placing on the market for cultivation of genetically modified maize 1507 (2016/2920(RSP)) (B8-1085/2016);

—   Objection pursuant to Rule 106: Renewing the authorisation for the placing on the market for cultivation of genetically modified maize MON 810 (2016/2921(RSP)) (B8-1086/2016);

—   Objection pursuant to Rule 106: Renewing the authorisation for the placing on the market of genetically modified maize MON 810 (2016/2922(RSP)) (B8-1084/2016);

—   Objection pursuant to Rule 106: Placing on the market of products containing, consisting of, or produced from genetically modified cotton 281-24-236 × 3006-210-23 × MON 88913 (2016/2923(RSP)) (B8-1088/2016).
