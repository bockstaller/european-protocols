3. Composition of Parliament

Barry Madlener had given notice in writing of his resignation as Member of Parliament, with effect from 20 September 2012.

Pursuant to Rule 4(1), Parliament established the vacancy with effect from that date and informed the national authorities concerned.

The Netherlands authorities had given notice of the election of Patricia van der Kammen to replace Barry Madlener as Member of Parliament with effect from 3 October 2012.

Under Rule 3(2), until such time as her credentials had been verified or a ruling had been given on any dispute, and provided that she had previously made a written declaration stating that she did not hold any office incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament, Patricia van der Kammen would take her seat in Parliament and on its bodies and would enjoy all the rights attaching thereto.

7. Interpretation of the Rules of Procedure

The President informed the House, under Rule 211(3), of the following interpretation of Rule 191(1), provided by the Committee on Constitutional Affairs, which had been consulted on the application of that provision:

'Only full members of a committee, elected in accordance with Article 186, may be elected to the bureau of that committee.'

If this interpretation was not contested by a political group or by at least forty Members (Rule 211(4)) before the opening of the next day's sitting (Tuesday, 23 October 2012), it would be deemed to have been adopted. Otherwise, the matter would be put to the vote in Parliament.
