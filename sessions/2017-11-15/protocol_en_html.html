2. Negotiations ahead of Parliament's first reading (consent) (Rule 69c)

The President announced that he had received a request for a vote from enough Members to constitute the medium threshold on the LIBE Committee's decision to enter into interinstitutional negotiations on the basis of the Report on the proposal for a regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council establishing the criteria and mechanisms for determining the Member State responsible for examining an application for international protection lodged in one of the Member States by a third-country national or a stateless person - Rapporteur: Cecilia Wikström (A8-0345/2017), announced in the minutes of Monday, 13 November 2017 (minutes of 13.11.2017, item 9).

The vote would be held the following day, 16 November 2017.

The President announced that he has received no request for a vote on the LIBE Committee's further decisions to enter into interinstitutional negotiations, as announced in the minutes of Monday, 13 November 2017 (minutes of 13.11.2017, item 9).

The LIBE Committee had therefore been able to initiate the negotiations on expiry of the deadline laid down in Rule 69(c)(2).
