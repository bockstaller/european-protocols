1.  Opening of sitting

The sitting opened at 10.00.

The following spoke: Richard Corbett , who pointed out that Luis Herrero-Tejedor and Michael Henry Nattrass had still not yet updated their declarations of financial interests even though the fact that they had failed to do so had already been mentioned several times in the Minutes ( Minutes of 23.04.2007, Item 14 , Minutes of 09.05.2007, Item 5 and Minutes of 21.05.2007, Item 12 ). 13.  Agenda

At its meeting that morning, the Conference of Presidents had decided to propose the following changes to the agenda for the sittings of 06 and 07.06.2007.

Wednesday 06.06.2007

- a statement by the High Representative for CFSP was placed on the agenda after the report by Enrique Barón Crespo and Elmar Brok on the roadmap for the European Union's constitutional process (Item 43 on the agenda) ;

The motions for a resolution tabled to wind up the statement would be put to the vote during the next part-session in Strasbourg.

- the report by Godelieve Quisthoudt-Rowohl on EU economic and trade relations with Russia would be held over until the June part-session in Strasbourg;

- the report by María Isabel Salinas García (A6-0183/2007 ) on specific rules as regards the fruit and vegetable sector, which was scheduled to be put to the vote under Rule 131 on Thursday, 07.06.2007, was placed at the end of Wednesday's agenda, with the tabling of amendments.

Thursday 07.06.2007 (Voting time)

- the report by Jacek Saryusz-Wolski on the conclusion of the Protocol to the Partnership and Cooperation Agreement between the European Communities and their Member States, of the one part, and the Russian Federation, of the other part, to take account of the accession of the Republic of Bulgaria and Romania to the European Union (A6-0192/2007 ) would be taken under Rule 43(1);

- the report by James Elles on draft amending budget No 3/2007 (formerly 4) concerning the surplus of the 2006 financial year (A6-0196/2007 ) would be taken under Rule 131;

- the report by Carlos Coelho on the application of the provisions of the Schengen acquis relating to the Schengen Information System in the Czech Republic, the Republic of Estonia, the Republic of Latvia, the Republic of Lithuania, the Republic of Hungary, the Republic of Malta, the Republic of Poland, the Republic of Slovenia and the Slovak Republic (A6-0204/2007 ) would be taken under Rule 131.

The House agreed to the changes.

17.  Membership of Parliament

Vasile Puşcaş had given notice of his resignation as an MEP with effect from 02.05.2007.

Bernat Joan i Marí had given notice of his resignation as an MEP with effect from 19.06.2007.

In line with Rule 4(1), Parliament established the two vacancies as of the above-mentioned dates and informed the national authorities concerned.

The Romanian authorities had given notice of the appointment of Rovana Plumb to replace Vasile Puşcaş as Member of Parliament with effect from 02.05.2007.

The French authorities had given notice of the appointment of Elisabeth Morin to replace Roselyne Bachelot-Narquin as Member of Parliament with effect from 24.05.2007.

Under Rule 3(2), until such time as their credentials had been verified or a ruling had been given on any dispute, and provided that they had previously made a written declaration stating that they did not hold any office incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament, Rovana Plumb and Elisabeth Morin would take their seats in Parliament and on its bodies and would enjoy all the rights attaching thereto.
