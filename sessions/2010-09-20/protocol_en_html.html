IN THE CHAIR: Jerzy BUZEK
President

1. Resumption of the session

The sitting opened at 17.05.


2. Tribute

On behalf of Parliament, the President paid tribute to the memory of Egon A. Klepsch, President of the European Parliament from 1992 to 1994.

Parliament observed a minute's silence.


3. Announcement by the President

The President spoke about the possible consequences of the strike announced for Thursday.

The following spoke: John Bufton and Francesco Enrico Speroni.


4. Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting

The minutes of the previous sitting were approved.

The following spoke: Bruno Gollnisch on the basis of Articles 172 and 151 of the Rules of Procedure, to deplore the fact that no roll-call vote had taken place on the joint motion for a resolution on the situation of the Roma in Europe (RC-B7-0493/2010) (minutes of 9.9.2010, item 5.2) and to make a personal statement following what had been said by Jean-Pierre Audy and Véronique Mathieu on this matter.


5. Composition of Parliament

The Hungarian authorities had given notice of the election of Zoltán Bagó to replace Enikő Győri as Member of Parliament with effect from 12 September 2010.

Under Rule 3(2), until such time as his credentials had been verified or a ruling had been given on any dispute, and provided that he had previously made a written declaration stating that he did not hold any office incompatible with that of Member of the European Parliament, Zoltán Bagó would take his seat in Parliament and on its bodies and would enjoy all the rights attaching thereto.
