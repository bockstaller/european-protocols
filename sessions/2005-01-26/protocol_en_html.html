5.  Agenda

The order of business had been established ( Minutes of 10.01.2005, Item 10 ) and a corrigendum to the agenda had been distributed (PE 352.950/OJ/COR ).

The President pointed out that Members would have the chance to make one-minute speeches under Rule 144 the following morning, between the end of the formal sitting and 10.00.

The agenda was thus established.
