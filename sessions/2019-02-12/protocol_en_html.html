2. Negotiations ahead of Parliament's first reading (Rule 69c)

The President announced the decision by the relevant committee to enter into interinstitutional negotiations on the basis of the following report (pursuant to Rule 69c (1)):

—   LIBE Committee: proposal for a regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council on the European Border and Coast Guard and repealing Council Joint Action No 98/700/JHA, Regulation (EU) No 1052/2013 of the European Parliament and of the Council and Regulation (EU) No 2016/1624 of the European Parliament and of the Council (COM(2018)0631 – C8-0406/2018 – 2018/0330(COD)). Rapporteur: Roberta Metsola (A8-0076/2019).

In accordance with Rule 69c (2), Members or political group(s) reaching at least the medium threshold may request in writing, before midnight the following day, for the decision to enter into negotiations to be put to the vote.

Negotiations may start at any time after that deadline has expired if no request for a vote in Parliament on the decision to enter into negotiations has been made.
